Dhanashree Verma is a dentist, choreographer, and YouTuber.

Dhanashree Verma age

Dhanashree was born on 27th September 1996 in Dubai to Kapil Sharma and Varsha Verma.

Dhanashree’s father Kapil Sharma is a businessman based in Dubai while her mother Varsha Verma is a housewife.

Dhanashree Verma Dance

Apart from being a dentist, Dhanashree is also very interested in dancing and as a result, she enrolled in Shiamak Davar dance classes, where she got dance training under famous Bollywood choreographer Shiamak Davar.

Eventually, Dhanashree began making dancing videos and posted them on social media platforms.

As fate would have it, she became popular on social media, and later she started her own career as a professional dance choreographer.
