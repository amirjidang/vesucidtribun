Cameron Diaz is an actress and former model who has accumulated a substantial fortune throughout her successful career. Diaz rose to prominence in the 1990s as a co-star in The Mask with Jim Carrey. She established herself as a leading actress in both comedy and drama genres. Cameron Diaz's contributions to the entertainment industry, her notable film roles, and her status as a fashion icon have made her a beloved and respected figure in the corridors of Hollywood. In addition to her film career, she has also worked as a model. In her teens, she began modeling and participating in advertisements for companies like Calvin Klein and Levi's. Her modeling experience helped launch her acting career, and she continues to be regarded as a style icon. Diaz has accumulated her $140 million fortune through these careers and other business ventures.

Earns: Acting career

Cameron Diaz's film career spans over four decades and includes various roles in various genres. She has made a name for herself as a gifted actress, renowned for her charisma, comic timing, and capacity to give her characters depth. Diaz has a successful acting career and starred in over 50 movies, contributing significantly to her income. Some of her successful films include There's Something About Mary, Charlie's Angels, and The Mask, which would have provided her with substantial salaries, bonuses, and residuals.

Spends: Real Estate Investments

Cameron Diaz is known to invest in real estate properties, particularly luxurious and high-end residences. Diaz has invested a portion of her fortune in luxury properties. These include high-end homes in desirable locations, both for personal use and as investment properties. According to architecturaldigest, Diaz sold her Hollywood home in 2022 to Ariana Grande for $4.9 million. Diaz and Benji also own a 1.75-acre mansion in Montecito, California, which they acquired for $12.67 million.

Earns: Endorsements and Brand deals

Diaz has also generated her income by endorsing products and collaborating with brands. She has secured lucrative endorsement deals and partnerships with various companies as a recognizable and influential figure. Diaz has served as the brand ambassador for Tag Heuer, a luxury Swiss watchmaker. She has also appeared in their advertising campaigns, promoting their timepieces. Other brands she has participated in include Coca-Cola, Lux shampoo and SoftBank Mobile, among many others. It's worth noting that Diaz's financial portfolio has grown as a result of these endorsements.

Earns: Production

Cameron Diaz has ventured into film production in addition to her acting career. She co-founded the production company A Lighthouse Entertainment and has produced films such as The Sweetest Thing and Bad Teacher. These endeavors have generated additional income. While her producing credits may not be as extensive as her acting roles, she has been involved in several projects as a producer.

Earns: Book Publishing

Cameron Diaz expanded her creative endeavors beyond acting and into book publishing. Diaz has authored health, well-being, and self-care books, such as The Body Book and The Longevity Book. Her two books reflect Diaz's passion for health, wellness, and personal growth. They combine her personal experiences, research, and expert advice to give readers insights and practical tips for leading a healthy and fulfilling life. The popularity of these works has added to her revenue, with millions of copies sold.

Spends: Travel

Cameron Diaz has had the opportunity to travel extensively throughout her career, both for work-related commitments and personal enjoyment. Additionally, she has been known to enjoy leisure travel and has been spotted vacationing in different parts of the world. She has been enjoying beach destinations, such as Hawaii and the Caribbean, where she can relax and unwind from her busy schedule.

Earns: Business Ventures

While Cameron Diaz is primarily known for her acting career, she has also ventured into business and entrepreneurial endeavors. Diaz co-founded Avaline Wine in 2020 along with entrepreneur Katherine Power. Avaline is a brand that focuses on producing clean, organic, and transparently labeled wines. The brand offers a variety of white, rose, and red wines made from organic grapes. Diaz has expressed a passion for health and wellness, which has led her to explore organic and eco-friendly living. Cameron Diaz is also a keynote speaker and industry expert who speak on themes such as Entertainers, Television & Film, and Health, and she receives over $200,000 for each event.
