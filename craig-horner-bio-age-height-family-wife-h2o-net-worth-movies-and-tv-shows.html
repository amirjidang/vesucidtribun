Craig Horner Biography

Craig Horner is an Australian Actor, Musician and Producer who was born and brought-up in Brisbane, Queensland. He is widely known for his role in the television series “Legend of the Seeker” as Richard Cypher, the fantasy series “H2O: Just Add Water” as Ash Dove and in the Fantasy drama television series “Once Upon a Time” as The Count of Monte Cristo. He made his debut in “Cybergirl” portraying Jackson Campbell and later he appeared in “Blurred” as Pete the Bus Nerd.
