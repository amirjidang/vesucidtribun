Publish date: 2024-08-06

The former The Daily Show host is still the most eligible bachelor in Hollywood. Trevor Noah has been in and out of relationships since 2014. Trevor Noah has been under fire for his political commentaries. Regarding dating, he keeps it eccentric and exciting for the tabloids.From being committed to a physiotherapist, then moving on to a real estate agent, and finally an actress, the reality host has kept most of his love life under wraps. The latest buzz is that Trevor Noah is dating Albanian singer Dua Lipa. They were caught lip-locking on a dinner date in New York. Let us trace all the women Trevor Noah has dated before falling for the sultry pop sensation.

8 Trevor Noah And Dani Gabriel Met In 2014

Tied geographically, they both belonged to South Africa. This connection brought Trevor Noah and Dani Gabriel closer in 2014. Dani was a practicing physiotherapist located in Cape Town. She was a supportive partner who cheered him on his public comedy club gigs and tours worldwide. The duo were seen getting amorous on luxurious vacations and had a good time in each other's company.

7 When Trevor Noah And Dani Gabriel Split

The relationship lasted over a year; Trevor meanwhile got an offer to shift his base permanently to New York as the new host of The Daily Show.

Distance played the culprit between the two love birds, since Dani did not want to halt her physiotherapy practice in Cape Town and move to NY. She, however, felt proud of his accomplishments and wished him well. They split amicably in 2015.

6 Trevor Noah Met Jordyn Taylor In 2015

The comedian met model and real estate agent Jordyn Taylor after he moved to the United States to begin his stint as a coveted TV show host. They made their relationship public in November 2015. The hot couple was papped together while attending the MTV Movie and TV awards in 2017.

They didn’t shy away from occasional PDA while celebrating New Year's Eve, going on mushy dates, and enjoying Formula E Race. The compatible duo also indulged in adventure sports and traveled to exotic destinations. Paparazzi and fans quickly noticed that the leggy beauty shared an uncanny resemblance to Noah’s ex Dani Gabriel.

5 When Trevor Noah And Jordyn Taylor Spilt Up

The three-year-long relationship came to an end in 2018. The reason was never known to the public, however, while taking on a live Instagram Q&A with her fans, the beautiful model announced the split.

Jordyn Taylor mentioned that Trevor Noah has quietly broken off with her. But she did confess that he would always remain in her heart as a good friend.

4 Trevor Noah Met Minka Kelly In August 2020

Sparks were flying high for the Euphoria actress and the comedian in August 2020 when rumors of the duo dating came out in the media. This was confirmed to the public subtly when they were photographed together outside Noah’s apartment in New York in September 2020. The Daily Show host soon purchased a $27.5 million mansion for the couple to live in together.

In May 2021, they were spotted cuddling and kissing on a romantic vacation at St. Barts. Their fans rejoiced when the duo officially addressed their dating over Instagram while vacationing with family and friends in South Africa in December 2021. The media and fans were sure that Noah would soon settle down with the charming actress.

3 When Did Trevor Noah Break Up With Minka Kelly?

The duo had temporarily separated in May 2021 and had tried to work on their relationship. Trevor Noah and Minka Kelly briefly reunited, and things were going well when precisely a year later, in May 2022, they officially called it quits.

The reason was Trevor Noah’s workaholic nature and his hectic schedule. The Friday Nights Lights actress said she didn't want to waste her time and energy behind the wrong guy anymore.

2 Trevor Noah Spends Time With Dua Lipa In September 2022

Fans and media were visibly surprised when pictures of the comedian and pop sensation were leaked in September 2022. Trevor Noah and Minka Kelly were seen engaged in a steamy lip lock on the streets of New York after their romantic date at Miss Lily’s in East Village. It appeared that Noah’s charm smote the Levitating singer as he acted like the perfect gentleman.

1 Are Trevor Noah And Dua Lipa Still A Thing?

After blowing up the tabloids two months before, the rumored to-be dating couple have kept their fans guessing about their status. In an interview, the comedian has laughed off the said relationship with the Hallucinating singer. He termed their bond as good friends. Lipa also opened up in the latest episode of Dua Lipa: At Your Service podcast and called the tabloid news baseless. She confirmed being single.

Trevor Noah seems to be an ambition-driven individual who doesn't have much time to be in a serious relationship. The TV host has had his fair share of love escapades, even though he keeps things strictly tongue-tied.
