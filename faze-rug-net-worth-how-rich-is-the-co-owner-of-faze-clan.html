FaZe Rug Early Life

Brian Rafat Awadis, aka FaZe Rug, was born on November 19, 1996 in San Diego, California. His parents, Ron and Sana Awadis, are Iraqi immigrants. Ron has two stores in San Diego.

Brian and Brandon started their YouTube account in 2008. Brian launched his channel at the age of twelve. After a few films with his brother, the channel went dormant in 2009.

Despite this, his passion for YouTubing remained. In high school, he created a second channel and posted there throughout high school and early college. He’d go to San Diego Miramar College but leave out his first year to work on content.
