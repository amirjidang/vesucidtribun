Kanye West's Music Gets A Classical Remix From A Young Pianist

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Kanye West‘s music, sonically, just reached new heights. Known for his groundbreaking instrumentals and knack for utilizing just the right sample to boast a certain type of soul, Mr. West solidified himself as one of music’s most innovative producers. Now, a few of his tracks from his prized discography received a remixed coating in a classical way.

A musician by the name of Young Piano decided to exercise his fingers on the keys by playing a handful of songs from Yeezy’s projects, and did it without taking a break to crack his knuckles. A few of the melodies that made the cut include “Through The Wire,” “Amazing,” “Bound 2” and The Life Of Pablo‘s “Wolves.”

With the Chicagoan planning to release his eighth solo studio album, Turbo Grafx 16 later this year, Young Piano, 22, might have to add another track to his viral video talent.
