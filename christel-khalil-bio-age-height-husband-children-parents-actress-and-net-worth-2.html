Christel Khalil The Young And The Restless

Khalil played the recurring role of Lily Winters in The Young and The Restless,  is an American television soap opera created by William J. Bell and Lee Phillip Bell for CBS.

On June 23, 2012, Khalil won the Daytime Emmy Award for Outstanding Younger Actress in a Drama Series after four nominations in the category at the 39th Daytime Emmy Awards.

In August 2018, Christel announced her decision to run down from being on contract with the show and instead proceed on a recurring basis. Khalil explained the decision was pushed by her desire to have more freedom in both her professional and personal life.

Khalil continued to make intermittent appearances on the show until being approached by current head writer and co-executive producer Josh Griffith in January 2020, to return to filming on a regular basis.
