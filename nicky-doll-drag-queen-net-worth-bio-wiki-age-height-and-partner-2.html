Nicky Doll Biography

Karl Sanchez, famously known by her stage name Nicky Doll is a French-American Drag queen known for her participation in RuPaul’s drag race. Nicky describes herself as ” a ’90s inspired, a life-sized doll based in New York,” whose edgy fashion appearance is influenced by myriad stylistic movements, ranging from Parisian underground nightlife scene to the J-pop colorful culture.

Growing up as a young queer kid while living in Morocco was definitely not easy, which is why Karl tried to find an escape into her own fantasy world through poetry, watching anime, and video games. Growing up she was a huge J-Pop fan growing up. She is a trained make up artist.

Nicky Doll likes to be involved in fashion editorials and diverse visual projects. Recently she did the cover of Féroce Magazine and has been featured as a model on various publications such as En vie Magazine (USA), OUT magazine, Lash Magazine (France), as well as Cosmopolitan. After venturing out of the Parisian party scene and relocating to the United States in 2015. In Sanfransico she left a lasting impression when she made her debut at notorious Black XXXMas (Industry + Gus Presents). She earned dancing opportunities at various world-class venues from The Abbey in Los Angeles to Queen Club Champs-Élysées in Paris due to her undeniable stage presence, captivating dance moves, and sparkling persona. She has shared the stage with various A-list DJs such as Tommy Love, Gus Presents, Abel, Nina Flowers, and Bob Sinclair just to name a few.
