When everyone was busy celebrating the 4th of July, a family was mourning the death in Tennessee of their 10-year-old daughter, Kaylee Hunt, who died in a boating accident over the weekend.

On Saturday, 15th October 2022, Kylee Isabella Hunt passed tragically and unexpectedly due to the injuries incurred in a boating accident, according to an online obituary.

According to sources, the little girl was only ten years old when she lost her life as a passionate softball player.

Over the weekend of 4th of July 2022, Tennessee Family members in Decatur Country took made use of their long weekend by relaxing on the Tennessee River, but who knew things would turn ugly for them?

They lost their daughter in a boating accident. Currently, her family members are going through a wave of sadness.

Kaylee Hunt was a kind young girl. Everyone adored her because of her loving and caring nature.

‘She was exceptional. She was simply exceptional in every aspect. Kaylee moved to the rhythm of a different drum; she entered a room grinning and made everyone around her happy.
