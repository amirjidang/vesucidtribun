How many times has Clemson won the national championship?

Publish date: 2024-08-11

The Clemson Tigers are the athletic teams that represent Clemson University. They are located in Clemson, South Carolina.

The Clemons Tigers compete as a member of the National Collegiate Athletic Association (NCAA) Division I level (Football Bowl Subdivision (FBS) sub-level for football).

They have been competing in the Atlantic Coast Conference for all sports. Clemson have won multiple National Collegiate Athletic Football (NCAA) Division I national championships ranging from minor league ACC football, men’s soccer, and men’s golf.

Clemson University has three team national championships awarded by the NCAA. Clemson has also won three Division I Football National Championships, in 1981, 2016, and 2018. The most recent in 2018 saw a victory over Alabama.

Dan Radakovich is the athletic director. Clemons have 19 varsity teams and their colours are orange and regalia.

The men’s soccer team was Clemson’s second sports program to win a national championship, winning the NCAA Tournament in 1984 and again in 1987
