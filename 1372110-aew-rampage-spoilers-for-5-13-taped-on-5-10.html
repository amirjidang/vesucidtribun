AEW Rampage Spoilers For 5/13 (Taped On 5/10)

Publish date: 2024-08-17

After the May 10 episode of AEW Dynamite from the Little Caesars Arena in Detroit, Michigan, All Elite Wrestling taped matches for the May 13 episode of AEW Rampage.

The spoilers, courtesy of the Wrestling Observer and Mike from Indied, are as follows:

  • The Acclaimed (Max Caster & Anthony Bowens) & Billy Gunn def. Kip Sabin, The Butcher & The Blade.
  • Toni Storm def. Allysin Kay
  • Kyle Fletcher def. Action Andretti
  • The Hardy Boyz (Matt & Jeff Hardy) and Isiah Kassidy came out to do a promo when they were interrupted by The Gunns. The Gunns said they aligned with Ethan Page, and they wanted a match. Hardy replied by saying he would agree to the match if he gets control of Page’s contract if they win. The Gunns accepted the stipulation.
  • Mogul Embassy (Brian Cage and Swerve Strickland) def. John Silver & Alex Reynolds

WrestleZone will have AEW Rampage coverage as it airs on May 13.

AEW Dynamite Results (5/10/23): Jon Moxley vs. Kenny Omega
