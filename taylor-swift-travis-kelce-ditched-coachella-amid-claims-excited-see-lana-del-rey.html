Why Did Taylor Swift Not Attend The Coachella Music Festival?

Taylor Swift was not spotted at the Coachella Music Festival on April 12, 2024, because she chose to have dinner with her boyfriend, Travis Kelce, instead. This sighting surprised many fans who were expecting her to attend, especially because her friend, Lana Del Rey, was performing.

Of course, this didn't happen, but the couple has worked hard to spend as much time together despite their schedules. "Taylor is very excited about the opportunities Travis is getting and fully supports him," another source added, "[But] she wants him to be conscious of his schedule. The only issue is making sure they can see each other."

What Has Been Said About Travis Kelce And Taylor Swift's Relationship?

Fans who closely follow Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift's relationship will not be surprised they chose a quiet night rather than a public event. The couple has been incredibly supportive of each other, but after Kelce's Super Bowl win and the end of the first leg of Swift's Eras Tour, they have been enjoying some downtime.

In March 2024, a source told Us Weekly the pair were in their "nesting" phase. "They're focused on rest and recuperation and relaxing at home and having low-key and chill time together," a source said. "They are enjoying movie nights in her home theater, catching up on films and shows they've missed." "They can see each other as much as possible," the source said.
