Melissa Joan Hart Spouse: Who Is Melissa Joan Hart Husband?

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Melissa Joan Hart is married to American singer Mark Wilkerson.

Wilkerson is the lead vocalist and guitarist for the rock band Course of Nature and contributed to the lyrics of the song “It’s Not Over”.

Hart and Wilkerson met in 2002 and got married in 2003. They have three sons and the entire family live in Nashville.

Who Is Melissa Joan Hart?

Melissa Joan Hart is an actress Hart is best known for her child acting roles in the sitcoms “Clarissa Explains It All” and “Sabrina the Teenage Witch.”

Born on April 18, 1976, in New York, Hart began acting at age four and appeared in 25 TV commercials before she turned five.

In 1985, Hart appeared in the miniseries Kane & Abel, The Equalizer (1986), and Christmas Snow (1986).

Other films she has starred include Melissa & Joey (2010–2015), No Good Nick (2019), Drive Me Crazy (1999), Nine Dead (2009), God’s Not Dead 2 (2016), and Dirty Little Secret (2022).

Hart doubles as a film director, with the films The Goldbergs, “Hail Barry” and the Young Sheldon episode “Cowboy Aerobics and 473 Grease-Free Bolts” credited to her.

Hart made news recently when she surfaced as one of the people that helped rescue children during the Nashville school shooting.

According to her, she was close to Nashville’s Covenant School during the incident.
