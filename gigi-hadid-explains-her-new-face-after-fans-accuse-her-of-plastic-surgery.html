Gigi Hadid Explains Her New Face' After Fans Accuse Her Of Plastic Surgery

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Plastic surgery rumors run rampant in the world of Hollywood, but model Gigi Hadid is putting the latest ones to rest: Fans have accused the new mom of going under the knife after seeing her latest photo spread.

The 25-year-old supermodel graces the cover of Vogue's March issue, the first time Hadid has ever been featured on the prestigious fashion and lifestyle magazine solo. During her time with Vogue, Hadid also shared a Beauty Secrets video and her new-mom beauty routine.

While discussing her makeup tips and tricks, the model addressed the rumors about her getting plastic surgery, insisting that she has "never" had any work done or altered her face. As she explained during the interview, "That's the power of makeup."

"People pull up pictures [of me in high school] and they're like, 'Oh, Gigi's nose looks different in these pictures now.' Or they'll talk about something with my face and be like, 'It's changed on Gigi,'" Hadid shared in her video.

She continued, "I've never done anything to my face, but the way that I've learned to contour in certain places, to put bronzer in some places and leave it out in other places, that's something that you have to learn."

This is not the first time these rumors have cropped up around Hadid; she had to dispel other plastic surgery rumors during a Maybelline New York Instagram Live in May, after fans accused her of getting fillers in her face.

The model stated, "People think I do fillers on my face and that's why my face is round. I have had cheeks since I was born." She also went on to explain that her face had gotten rounder as a result of her pregnancy.

The new mom welcomed her daughter, Khai, with her boyfriend, Zayn Malik in September 2020.
