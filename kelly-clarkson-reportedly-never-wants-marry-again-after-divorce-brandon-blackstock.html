Fans are thrilled to see Kelly Clarkson thriving after her divorce from Brandon Blackstock. Not only is her television career continuing to thrive, but the singer has gotten fans talking with her very clear body transformation, which Kelly has attributed to a variety of lifestyle changes.

While the American Idol winner seems to be doing better than ever, don’t expect Kelly to settle down anytime soon. According to a new report, the mom-of-two isn’t looking for a serious relationship and may even be closed off to marriage given the roller coaster of emotions she went through with her ex.

Kelly Is Reportedly Telling People She’s Okay With Never Marrying Again

"She's in no rush to be in another relationship," a source shared, OK! Magazine reports. "She doesn't yearn for a life partner and has even said she had never been sexually attracted to anyone before Brandon."

Last March, a different source said something similar to OK! And even insisted that Kelly doesn’t see herself walking down the aisle again.

“Kelly has joked about the fact that she’s going to be single forever, but at the same time, she realizes forever is a long time,” they explained. “Although she truly doesn’t see herself dating or getting married again, her friends have encouraged her to at least be open to the idea of putting herself out there. However, it’s just not where her head is at right now.”

The source said that Kelly is enjoying feeling free post-divorce, adding, “Kelly’s able to come and go as she pleases, makes commitments only when she feels it truly aligns with her passions, and the only obligations she feels are towards her kids. Nothing comes before them.”

It's been two years since Kelly finalized her divorce from Brandon Blackstock, which resulted in her having to give him a million-dollar payout in addition to alimony and child support, though she has primary custody of their two kids. Their divorce was dragged out for years, even though Kelly initially filed in 2020, as the exes couldn’t reach a settlement despite having signed a prenup.

Kelly Admitted Last Year She Hasn’t Started Dating Again

The report echoes a sentiment that Kelly herself shared last year, when the television host admitted she’s not ready to start dating and it hasn’t even been something on her mind.

"I haven't even thought about it. Not that I'm against it. I'm not like bitter or, ‘Oh, I'm still broken,’” she explained. "I'm not mad at all. I'm very happy. I'm in a great place."

The “Breakaway” singer said she’s enjoying finding herself again after their six-year marriage, explaining, "But you know, I've lost a bit of me, like in my previous relationship and I love having her back." Kelly added, "I'm enjoying my children and I'm enjoying my work."
