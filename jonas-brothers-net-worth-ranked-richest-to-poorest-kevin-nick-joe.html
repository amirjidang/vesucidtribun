4 Joe Jonas: The Second-Richest Jonas Brother

Net Worth: $50 Million Net Worth

After The Jonas Brothers 'broke up', Joe went on a solo tour and put out his first album, which was called Fastlife.

Joe Jonas was also the lead singer in another band called DNCE. Most people remember them for their incredibly catchy tune, "Cake by the Ocean." Even though they eventually disbanded, they reunited here and there and ended up putting out some additional music.

Unfortunately, it didn't really go anywhere, and compared to his fame with his brothers, his experience with DNCE didn't rank at all.

Joe Jonas' net worth stands at $50 million. And because he and Sophie Turner did sign a prenup before getting married, most of his net worth should stay with him after the pending divorce.

Joe Jonas' Personal Life

Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner married in 2019

In May 2019, Joe Jonas married Sophie Turner. The couple have two daughters together. Willa, born in 2020, and Delphine, born in 2022.

Joe filed for divorce from Sophie in September 2023. While there have been moments of contention between the two, including Joe allegedly taking the children's passports so they could not travel to the UK and Sophie suing Joe for wrongful retention as a result, it appears that as of March 2024, the two have turned a corner. A custody agreement has been made via mediation, that both Joe and Sophie are happy with. Now, Joe and Sophie are moving forward with the divorce to bring it to an end in the near future.

2 Kevin Jonas: The Third-Richest Jonas Brother

Net Worth: $40 Million Net Worth

Kevin Jonas falls in line as the third of the Jonas Brothers' net worth, but his net worth is still very impressive.

Kevin stands out as a guitarist in The Jonas Brothers and is very talented at it, providing a great sound for his brothers' enchanted melodies. He was also the first of the brothers to be on reality TV, with the show Married to Jonas, which ran on E! from 2012 to 2013. The show gave fans a look into Kevin and Danielle Jonas' lives as a married couple when they weren't making music. The world was able to see them as newlyweds, how they interacted together and with each other's families, and a lot about Kevin that most fans don't know.

1 Kevin Jonas' Net Worth Grows With His Entrepreneur Spirit And Business Ventures

Kevin Jonas' business ventures include Nellie's Southern Kitchen restaurant, JonasWerner, and The Jonas Group

After the band parted ways, Kevin Jonas went into business for himself, founding JonasWerner, designing and building custom homes. Not only that, Kevin opened a restaurant called Nellie's Southern Kitchen in his hometown of Wyckoff, New Jersey, in 2012. The restaurant was named after his grandmother and served Southern-style food. It was a taste of Kevin's family history. Even though the restaurant closed in 2016, it was a good example of Kevin's willingness to try out different business ideas.

Then in 2020, Kevin joined forces with Phil McIntyre to start The Jonas Group, a talent management and entertainment company. The company wanted to give new artists full management services and help them figure out how to work in the complicated music industry. Kevin's past success as a musician made him an important part of this project.

Kevin Jonas' net worth is $40 million.

Kevin Jonas' Personal Life

Kevin Jonas and Danielle Deleasa have been married since 2014

Kevin Jonas is married to Danielle Deleasa. Since seeing them on Married to Jonas, Kevin and Danielle have welcomed two daughters. Their first daughter, Alena Rose Jonas, was born on February 2, 2014. Their second daughter, Valentina Angelina Jonas, was born on October 27, 2016.

Kevin Jonas and his wife Danielle work great together, and not just husband and wife, but as business partners. Danielle Jonas owns a luxury jewelry line called Danielle Jonas Co.

Kevin and Danielle also write children's books together, with two completed at this time called There's a Rock Concert in My Bedroom and There's a Beach in My Bedroom.
