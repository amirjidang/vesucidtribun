Who Is Sarah Rainford? 10 Facts About Molly Rainfords Mum

Strictly Come Dancing star Molly Rainford is the daughter of her father, Dave Rainford, and her mum, Sarah Rainford.

Molly Anne Rainford, widely known as Molly Rainford, is a famous English singer, actress, and television presenter. She has recently been a part of the BBC reality dancing show, Strictly Come Dancing. Molly was also the finalist in the sixth season of Britain’s Got Talent in 2012 and has gained a perfect amount of media exposure since then.

The singer was also one of the presenters of the Friday Download show. Molly has released two extended plays and is also portraying the title character in the CBBC comedy-drama series Nova Jones. Born on November 22, 2000, in the London Borough of Havering, she was later raised in Essex. She is also the daughter of popular English football midfielder David John Rainford or Dave Rainford.

Molly attended the Sylvia Young Theatre School and later East London Arts and Music to be more skillful in her major subjects of music and acting. At the age of 11, she auditioned for the sixth season of Britain’s Got Talent, where she sang her version of the song One Night Only, which paved her way further in the show, where she was later placed in the sixth position.

She gained a massive following and was then signed with Sony Music for her other musical career.
