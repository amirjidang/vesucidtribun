Who Are Beyonce Parents? All You Need To Know

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Beyonces parents, Tina and Mathew Knowles, have played an influential role in her success. Not only are they the proud founders of her success, but they have successful careers themselves.

Her father, Mathew Knowles, is celebrated for his business acumen and a long career in record production. In particular, Tina helped shape Beyoncé’s illustrious career as she designed outfits for Beyoncé’s performances in Destiny’s Child.

As a result of her proficient craftsmanship and signature style, Tina became an international fashion symbol by 1990. Besides being fashion designers, both parents showed great faith in their daughter and were always there to help her navigate the challenging paths of showbiz worldwide.

Following in her parent’s footsteps, Beyoncé Giselle Knowles has established herself as one of the greatest singers and songwriters of all time, winning numerous awards along the way.

It is safe to say that they have been instrumental in helping to spread Beyonce’s presence around the globe and make a name as one of modern music’s most indomitable forces.

Mathew Knowles and Celestine Anne, also known as Tina Knowles, celebrated their 70th and 68th birthdays, respectively, in 2023. Mathew was born on January 9, 1952, in Texas, and Tina was born on 4th January 1954.

His career started out with him working as a sales executive. However, he realized his dreams of entering the music industry when he discovered Destiny’s Child in 1990.

Mathew and Tina raised Beyonce and her younger sister, Solange Knowles, in Houston, Texas, and throughout their daughter’s successful careers, they have been incredibly supportive.

They have encountered personal and professional obstacles but remain immensely proud of Beyonce.

Beyonce Father Mathew 

Mathew Knowles is a highly accomplished businessperson and music manager, having won many awards, such as the Grammy Award, the American Music Award, and the BET Awards. 

He made revolutionary decisions when managing the careers of his daughters Beyoncé and Solange, as well as being heavily involved in the solo careers of Destiny’s Child departed members Michelle Williams and Kelly Rowland. 

His outstanding accomplishments in this field have enabled him to be celebrated today as one of the most talented entertainment managers in history.

Knowles has had a successful career in many areas of the entertainment industry. In 2009, he was one of the executive producers behind the film Obsessed, which starred his daughter Beyoncé.

Since then, he has also worked on two reality series Breaking From Above and The UK’s Chancers respectively. 

Recently, he was appointed to APX’s board of directors and is currently managing a large fund for co-productions between the U.S and Italy. His estimated net worth stands at a remarkable fifty million dollars. 

With such lofty accomplishments as an achievement credit, it is clear that Mathew Knowles is indeed an influential figure in today’s entertainment industry.

Beyonce’s Mother, Tina

Tina’s story is one of the humble beginnings and remarkable success. The daughter of a longshoreman and seamstress from Galveston, Texas, Tina picked up her mother’s craft at an early age and started making dresses while in high school. 

However, it was the success of Destiny’s Child in the 1990s that propelled her into the public eye as she became well-known for creating iconic stage outfits for the girl group. 

Rather than rest on her laurels, Tina opened the Miss Tina apparel line which focused on stylish designs for older women in various sizes–at reasonable prices. 

This bold move has undoubtedly paid off; Tina’s business interests now account for a large proportion of her estimated $25 million net worth. Truly an inspirational example of an industry trailblazer.
