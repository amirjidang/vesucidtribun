7 Most Expensive Celebrity Motorhomes Of All Time

Gone are the days when Motorhomes were only associated with travelers. Celebrities have been using these trailers to film on the road or use as a private space to relax after a long shoot. Celebrities have multi-million dollar property portfolios, expensive car collections, private jets, and yachts, and joining the list are motorhomes that have been popular with musicians and actors alike. While standard trailers offer regular amenities, some motorhomes are customized to the fullest, stacked with every possible feature required by the celebrity. Designing with the best materials and adding multiple stories adds millions of dollars to the cost.

From Jamie Foxx to Will Smith, the world’s highest-paid celebrities and musicians love to travel and live in style on the set. Their multi-level moving homes have become iconic for their features and prices. Let’s take a look at the most expensive celebrity motorhomes of all time.

Vin Diesel’s Tractor-Trailer

The tractor-trailer rig owned by Vin Diesel costs $1.1 million and features 1,000 sq. ft. of living space. According to Hot Cars, the two-story motorhome has $70,000 worth of electronics for an unforgettable surround sound experience. With a cozy living room and two-person dinner space on the bottom floor, the second floor has a kids’ play area for Diesel’s children when they visit him on set.

Jamie Foxx’s Off Script Trailer

Partnering with Great Goose Vodka and Anderson Mobile Estates, Jamie Foxx’s Off Script is one of the biggest celebrity trailers. It was made explicitly for Foxx’s talk show Off Script, and the RV goes by the same name. Foxx had a unique concept where he would travel to celebrities with his trailer and chat with them between their movie takes. The two-story motorhome comes with two slide-outs and a hydraulic pop-up roof. The lower level has a make-up room and a recording studio. Guests sit on the custom royal blue velvet couch for their interviews. It took four weeks to build the RV and cost upwards of $1.8 million.

Leonardo DiCaprio's Inception Trailer

Leonardo DiCaprio played the lead in Christopher Nolan’s 2010 action sci-fi, Inception. While filming the movie in 2009, DiCaprio used a mobile home that was truly one of a kind. Designed by King Kong Vehicles, the RV spans 53 feet and expands from 8 feet to 16 feet in width. The home has two fireplaces, heated floors, four large popouts, and five TVs, as noted by The Things. The open living room is stacked with multiple couches, while the kitchen is equipped with every essential amenity, including a marble countertop and barstools. The glass tiled shower in the bathroom costs $25,000 alone.

Mariah Carey’s The Lounge

Another creation by Anderson Mobile Estates, Mariah Carey’s two-story, three-level motor home, is a skyscraper on wheels. Purchased in 2015, the RV also comes with a hydraulic roof that can be pushed open with a button. Divided into three levels, the bottom level has a lounge area with make-up, the middle level has a kitchen, and the upper level features a lounge that can turn into a nightclub. The kitchen has custom countertops and a Kangen Water machine that costs $4,000. The customized lounge has a $7,000 resting inside. The RV cost the singer $1.8 million.

Simon Cowell’s X Factor Lounge

Simon Cowell is a judge on various reality shows. He owns a $2 million motorhome that he uses to catch a break between takes. The RV is also manufactured by Anderson Mobile Estates and comes with custom windows with electric shades and 26 speakers installed into the walls. The second floor of the two-story RV has a workspace and make-up area where a TV can pop up with a button, as noted by The Hollywood Reporter. The interior is made of Italian woodwork that suits the decor.

Justin Bieber’s Tour Bus

A rolling penthouse suite, Justin Bieber’s tour bus is the epitome of luxury. It features a plush bedroom, and next to it is an infrared sauna to relax in every night. The motorhome has multiple closet spaces throughout the RV that Bieber and his wife share. The massive living space has a large TV where the singer likes to watch sports games. The colorful LEDs around the ceiling can change lightning depending on the preference. The RV set him back by $2 million.

Willi Smith’s The Heat

As one of the most well-paid actors, Will Smith purchased a movie trailer to use when taking a break on the road. Made by the American RV Company Anderson Mobile Estates, Will Smith’s motorhome was the first big-budget RV made by them. As mentioned by Auto Evolution, they first built an RV called The Studio that Will Smith loved, so he customized it and named it The Heat. The two-story, 22-wheel RV cost him a whopping $2.5 million. Offering luxury amenities, the motorhome has $125,000 worth of tech and gadgets, including 14 TVs, $30,000 of leather on the couches and ceiling, and $200,000 of granite countertops.

Some other notable celebrities with expensive motorhomes include Brad Pitt, John Mayer, and Miranda Lambert. Celebrities like to wind down in style and comfort. As they live away from their homes for long periods, celebs need to add their personal touch to their temporary moving homes. As movie shootings and concert tours have resumed in 2021, more of these celebs will be seen living in their motorhomes again.
