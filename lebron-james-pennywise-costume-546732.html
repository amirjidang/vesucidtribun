LeBron James Dressed As Pennywise The Clown For Halloween

Publish date: 2024-08-30

There are a few celebrities that really take Halloween to another level- the Carters, Neil Patrick Harris and his family, and Heidi Klum to name a few. Basketball superstar LeBron James has also proven that he also has a knack for this fun holiday, and 2017 was another standout year for the small forward.

King James dressed as the movie villain of the year, Pennywise The Dancing Clown, from the film It. Not only did James commit with the freakish mask and full costume, but he even provided an eerie high-pitched voice, frightening laugh, a bevy of balloons and of course, dance moves. With his towering frame bumping up the level of creepiness, it’s safe to say that this year, LeBron shoots and he scores.

In previous years, James dressed as Jerome from Martin, Prince and Flo from the Progressive commercials. Check out his convincing and terrifying Pennywise costume below.
