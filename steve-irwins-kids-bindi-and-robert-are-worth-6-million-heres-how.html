Steve Irwin's Kids Bindi And Robert Are Worth $6 Million... Here's How

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Steve Irwin’s untimely death in 2006, which was caused by a stingray barb puncturing his chest, left many of his fans shocked and heartbroken. The worst affected was Irwin’s wife, Terri, and their kids, Bindi and Robert. At the time of the death of the superbly famous Australian wildlife expert and television personality, Bindi was eight years old, and Robert was 2. However, it soon became apparent that Steve’s family was keen on keeping the legacy alive. Terri, Bindi, and Robert have all contributed in unison towards that cause. They both made appearances on Steve’s television shows and even in public demonstrations with crocodiles on-site at the family-owned Australia Zoo. No wonder they have grown up in the public eye.

Bindi Irwin

Having hosted her first wildlife TV series, Bindi The Jungle Girl, at the age of just eight, she has also starred in the 2010 movie Free Willy: Escape from Pirate’s Cove, and hosted the Australian game show Bindi’s Bootcamp, centered around the theme of wildlife in 2012. She has also released several albums with her band Bindi and the Jungle Girls. In 2015, Bindi won the twenty-first season of Dancing With The Stars along with dancing partner Derek Hough. Interestingly, she had to receive special permission from a California judge to compete as a minor.

Eventually, she got engaged to her long-time boyfriend Chandler Powell; what’s more, she revealed in an Instagram post that Powell popped the note at Australia Zoo, and brother Robert captured the happy event in pictures. In an interview with Jimmy Fallon last year, Robert revealed that he would be the one to walk Bindi down the aisle. Her return to American TV, after a period of going under the radar, was on Celebrity Family Feud, where she, alongside her mom and brother, competed against actress Chrissy Metz and her relatives.

Robert Irwin

Known as a television personality, Robert is best known for starring in the reality V series Crikey! It’s the Irwins. He also hosted the TV series Wild But True for the Discovery Kids Channel. Another major accomplishment was his co-creation of the Robert Irwin: Dinosaur Hunter book series. His other noteworthy appearances include Tonight Show, where he appeared more than ten times, the film Free Willy: Escape from Pirate’s Cove (2010), etc. He also voiced a role in the Australian version of the movie The Sponge Bob Movie: Sponge Out Of Water in 2015. In 2013 he was nominated for a Logie Award for Most Popular New Male Talent.

Their Net Worth

While Bindi and Robert Irwin are undoubtedly rich in terms of happiness, we cannot help but wonder how rich they are in terms of actual money. According to Celebrity Net Worth, both Bindi and Robert have a total net worth of $3 million each. Reportedly, Terri and Steve have a net worth of $10 million, just in case you wanted to know.

Since 2018, Bindi and Robert, accompanied by their mother, have been hosting the Animal Planet show Crikey! It’s the Irwins, which is targeted at continuing Steve’s mission of bringing people closer to animals alive. Their dad must be immensely proud of them, and the conservation efforts they are putting in. We can also learn a thing or two from how close-knit the family has managed to remain over the years, despite Steve’s death.
