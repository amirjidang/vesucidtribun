The success of Shazam made it one of the most successful movies of 2019. DC’s Extended Universe finally found a film that received great reviews from critics and a generally positive source of entertainment for the fans. The movie made a whopping $365 million profiting off the $100 million budget to give DC a new top character. A mix of comedy and emotional storytelling gave the movie great depth to find success.

We will look at the lives of the actors to get casted in Shazam in terms of their net worth. The movie would see various characters played by two actors showing their children side and their superhero form as adults. Since most of the kids are now starting their careers, the adult actors to play the characters will be featured here. Find out which actors are making the most money. This is the official net worth rankings of the cast of Shazam.

Marta Milans: $1 million

Marta Milans scored the biggest role of her career as Rosa Vasquez in Shazam. The character is the foster mother of the main character Billy Batson and the rest of the kids at the foster home. Milans did a great job playing the likable and caring mother of the kids.

A character like this is clearly important in the overall story of the kids every day lives outside of the superhero scenes. Milans saw her career pick up steam thanks to the role in a hit film. The net worth of Milans is currently $1 million, and that will likely rise with the upcoming sequel.

Ross Butler: $1 million

The acting role for Ross Butler in Shazam sees him playing the foster brother of Billy named Eugene Choi. Butler is the adult superhero version known as Shazam Thunder. The character of Choi is known for his passions of gaming and keeping up with tech trends.

A fun mix of the new powers and his generally nerdy personality makes Choi one of the more appealing superheroes. Butler does a nice job in the role as his career continues to progress. The net worth of Butler is at $1 million which is quite impressive for a young actor.

Cooper Andrews: $2 million

Cooper Andrews was tasked with an important role in Shazam as Victor Vasquez. The foster father character sees him trying to welcome Billy into his new family. Victor has a comedic personality with a heart of gold that ensures the kids are always loved.

Andrews scored the great acting role to go along with his television gigs. Some of the more noteworthy roles include appearing on The Walking Dead and Halt and Catch Fire. The career of Andrews continues to ascend with an outstanding net worth of $2 million.

Michelle Borth: $3 million

The talented Michelle Borth plays the character of Mary Bromfield in Shazam to great success. Mary is Billy’s foster sister and the oldest of the foster siblings. The unique place character placement sees her taking a guardian role at times to help raise the younger kids.

Borth’s career has been going strong with other noteworthy roles in Hawaii Five-O and Tell Me You Love Me. The net worth of Borth is currently $3 million and growing. Borth is scheduled to return in the Shazam sequel bringing back her role as Lady Shazam.

D.J. Cotrona: $4 million

DJ. Cotrona gets to play the adult superhero alter ego of the character Pedro Pena in Shazam. Fans of the franchise loved the portrayal of Shazam Strong. Cotrona scored the great role that will keep him in the Shazam universe as long as the franchise continues.

Some of the other roles from his past includes appearances in films like G.I Joe: Retaliation and Dear John. Cotrona also had a regular lead role in the television series From Dusk till Dawn. The net worth of Cotrona is $4 million thanks to all his gigs making him quite valuable.

Mark Strong: $9 million

The success of Mark Strong made him one of the biggest names selected to become part of the Shazam cast. Strong played the lead villain character of Dr. Thaddeus Sivana looking to wreak havoc for Billy and his friends when they gain the superpowers.

Sivana was not chosen as a champion by Shazam missing out on the powers as a child. The career of Strong is impressive with noteworthy roles in hits like Sherlock Holmes, Zero Dark Thirty and Kick-Ass. Strong’s net worth is at $9 million as a top star for the Shazam franchise.

Meagan Good: $10 million

The career of Meagan Good started as a young age and has continued to find success. Shazam was one of her biggest movie acting roles playing the adult superhero version of Darla Dudley. The superhero alter ego of Shazam Lightning was quite beloved by the fans.

Good’s successful career has landed her a $10 million net worth. Some of the memorable acting roles for Good came in the films Think Like A Man, Anchorman 2: The Legend Continues and Saw V. Good was one of the bigger names casted for her role in Shazam to add some recognition to the cast.

Adam Brody: $12 million

Adam Brody is another well-known actor that joined the Shazam cast for an important role. The best friend of Billy is his new foster brother Freddy Freeman. Brody plays the adult version of Freeman as he adopts the superhero persona of Shazam Sky.

The career of Brody has been going strong with consistent roles most years. Some of the memorable performances came in The O.C. television show along with films like Thank You for Smoking and Mr. and Mrs. Smith. All the roles played a part in the tremendous $12 million net worth of Brody.

Djimon Hounsou: $12 million

The various hit movie roles of Djimon Hounsou helped make him a huge boost to the cast of Shazam. Hounsou played the ancient wizard character passing on the powers of Shazam to Billy. A long career of hit roles made Hounsou one of the most recognizable actors in the film.

Fans will remember him for the great work in films like Blood Diamond and Gladiator. Hounsou is also one of the few actors to have a presence in the Marvel Cinematic Universe and DC Extended Universe. The net worth of Hounsou is currently at $12 million.

Zachary Levi: $12 million

Zachary Levi scored the lead role of Billy in Shazam to represent the franchise well. This was the biggest role for Levi since the years spent as the lead star of NBC’s Chuck. Billy gains the powers of Shazam with Levi playing the adult superhero form.

The successful career of Levi earned him a net worth of $12 million tied for the best of the cast. Levi is another actor to appear in the MCU in Thor films before signing on to play Shazam in the DCEU. The net worth of Levi will likely rise with more Shazam movies planned with him as the face of the franchise.
