Pablo Milans Children: Meet Hayde Milans, Suyln Milans, Lian Milans, Lynn Milans, Anto

Publish date: 2024-08-27

Pablo Milanés Arias was a Cuban singer and guitarist. Pablo Milanes is the father of five children Haydee Milanes, Suylen Milanes, Lian Milanes, Lynn Milanes and Antonio Milanes.

Pablo Milanés died on November 22 2022, from cancer at the age of 79. At his family’s request, a wake will be held at the Casa de América cultural centre in Madrid on Wednesday, November 23 2022.

Meet Haydee Milanes

Haydee Milanes is a Cuban singer who is part of Cuba’s new fusion scene. Her style is often compared to that of Norah Jones: Shy, sultry, and jazzy.

Meet Suylen Milanes and Lian Milanes

Suylen also made a name for herself in Cuban culture in her fifties with an extensive music career. Lian Milanes is the twin sister of Suylen and she’s a music producer.

Meet Lynn Milanes

Lynn Milanes is also following in the footsteps of her father Pablo Milanes and is making a good name for herself when it comes to Cuban music, even though there isn’t any information about her career and all.

Meet Antonio Milanes

We know not much about Antonio Milanes aside from him being the brother of Haydee and we believe just as his siblings are successful, he is also successful though it’s obvious he didn’t do music.
