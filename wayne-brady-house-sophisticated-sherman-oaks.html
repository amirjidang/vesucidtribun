Wayne Brady House: Sophisticated Sherman Oaks

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Wayne Brady’s Sherman Oaks Home – Estimated Value - $1.8 Million

The famous comedian and star Wayne Brady owns a stylish home in Sherman Oaks, CA. It is a sophisticated house with a quaint touch – it is truly spectacular. See a few pictures of this property here.

It was constructed in 2004. Wayne purchased it later in 2005 for a whopping $2.550 Million.

A gourmet kitchen, a state-of–the-art home theater system, and a fanciful playhouse add a touch of uniqueness and style to this house. It features a grand entryway, living room, five bedrooms, a modern kitchen, a dining room, five bathrooms, a theater, and a patio. It comes with high ceilings, gorgeous moldings and designer touches that add style and beauty. The grand front entrance with a gated driveway makes it appear grand.

The entryway at the front leads to the main building. You will instantly see the arched windows, and as you enter you can see its hardwood floors and a romantic fireplace in the living quarters.

There is a wide proportionate master suite with a sitting area, a walk in closet, and a luxurious bathroom that gives a spa feel. The home theater with a gallery as the seating arrangement, and a curtain covered screen is truly the center of recreation and enjoyment.

The dining space is adjacent to the living room. It is fitted with custom lighting. The gourmet kitchen has granite counter tops, custom cabinets, stainless steel appliances, and a walk-in pantry.

There is a cozy patio at the back of the house. A dreamy whimsical playhouse is an added beauty. The vast stretching lush green yard is just perfect for stretching a leg or for relaxation.

Wayne has sold his lavish house recently for $1.8 Million on March 29th 2013. He wasn’t able to fetch the purchase price, and so incurred a loss of $ 350 Thousand.
