Julie Wilcox Obituary Funeral Notice Released

Publish date: 2024-08-20
  • The meteorologist Julie Wilcox died on Wednesday, 18th Jan.
  • The funeral will take place in the church on Thursday afternoon, i.e., 19th Jan, from 3:30 pm to 5:30 pm.
  • The burial services are scheduled to take place on Friday, i.e., 20th Jan, at 12:00 in the afternoon.

The family disclosed the details of Julie Wilcox’s obituary after she died on Wednesday, 18th January 2023.

The funeral will take place in the church on Thursday afternoon, i.e., 19th Jan, from 3:30 pm to 5:30 pm. The services will continue again from 7:00 in the evening until 9:00. 

Julie formerly worked at 13 news and was working as a meteorologist at the time of her death.

She was the daughter of Rosa Hubbard and shared a sibling named Cheryl Wilcox. Julie was also the mother of two children, Liam and Callum Wilcox.

The burial services are scheduled to take place on Friday, i.e., 20th Jan, at 12:00 in the afternoon. The burial will be performed in a Christian way.

Julie worked for multiple welfare causes. She raised money for the breast cancer foundation and was able to donate around $70,000 with the help of her friends and family. She also worked for SPCA for the cause of finding homes for stray animals in Hampton streets.

Julie’s fellow worker Evan Stewart from 13 news posted on her passing news, “For a dozen years, Julie was a member of our team. A skilled meteorologist and lover of animals, especially her cats. She found forever homes for countless furry friends with her weekly “Shelter Sunday” segments.”
