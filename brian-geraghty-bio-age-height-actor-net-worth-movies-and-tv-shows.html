Brian Geraghty Biography

Brian Timothy Geraghty was born on May 13, 1975. He is an American actor, known for his role alongside Denzel Washington in the 2012 film Flight and the HBO drama series Boardwalk Empire. He also appeared as a regular on NBC’S Chicago P.D from 2014 to 2016.

Brian Geraghty Education

He graduated from Toms River High School East in 1993 and then studied acting at the Neighborhood Playhouse School of Theatre.
