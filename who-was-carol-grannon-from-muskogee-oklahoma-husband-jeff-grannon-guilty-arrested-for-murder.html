Who Was Carol Grannon From Muskogee Oklahoma?

Carol Grannon was an Oklahoma resident from Muskogee. She suddenly disappeared on March 15, 1999. Later on, the authorities discovered that her husband brutally killed the mother of four by strangling her with a zip tie.

Carol Grannon Husband Jeff Grannon Found Guilty: Arrested For The Murder

After 12 years,  Jeffery Joseph Grannon pleaded guilty to first-degree murder in connection with his wife’s death. He confessed to strangling his wife with a zip-tie and placing her body in a sewer drain in an alley. The judge sentenced him to life in prison with parole after 15-years. 

The authorities had to spend three days to disinter the remains from the sewer drain. When they found the body, it was covered with a cement mix that had been poured into the 10- to 12-foot-deep drain.
