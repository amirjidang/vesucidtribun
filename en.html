Film Producer Grace Morton Parents Attend Her Destination Wedding With Matthew Budman

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Grace Morton parents Peter Morton and Tarlton Pauley met at an art gallery in 1986. Peter and Tarlton were married for seven years from 1990 to 1997.

Grace is an American production executive from Los Angeles, California. She is well-recognized as the VP of the Production and Development department of Bosque Ranch Productions. The company is owned by American actor-turned-filmmaker Taylor Sheridan.

Morton served as an associate producer for the 2021 movie, Those Who Wish Me Dead. Likewise, she has also worked as an assistant to Taylor in an episode of Yellowstone season 1 called The Remembering. 

Yellow is a neo-Western drama starring actors like Fin Little, Forrie J Smith, Brecken Merrill, and more.

Grace is currently making headlines after her lavish wedding ceremony with longtime partner Matthew Budman. The event, attended by many, A-listers, was held in Jamaica due to the venue’s history with both families.

Grace Morton Was Born To Peter Morton And Tarlton Pauley

Grace Morton parents Peter Morton and Tarlton Pauley were married from 1990 to 1997. The producer father Peter is the co-founder of the restaurant chain Hard Rock Cafe.

Peter and Tarlton were caught in a legal battle in 2007 when Grace’s mom sued her dad. Pauley alleged that her ex-husband fraudulently persuaded her to sell stock worth over $12.8 million for only $451 thousand. 

The lawsuit registered in LA County Superior Court stated that Morton also duped her by transferring her stock worth $29 million to his name.

Grace grew up alongside two brothers named Harry Morton and Matthew Morton. 

Matthew is the co-founder of companies like Happy Hour Drinks and Cha Cha Matcha. A graduate of NYU Stern School of Business, Matthew was once linked to American model Sofia Richie.

On the other hand, Harry was a restaurateur best known for founding Pink Taco. The restaurant chain currently has branches in Nevada, Arizona, LA, and Chicago. Harry passed away due to a cardiac arrest at his Beverly Hills residence; he was 38 years old at the time of his death on November 23, 2019.

Peter Morton

Grace Morton father Peter is an American businessman best known as the co-founder of the multinational restaurant chain called Hard Rock Cafe. 

He was born as the son of Arnie Morton and his first wife in Chicago, Illinois, on August 7, 1947. His dad Arnie was fourth of the restaurant company, Morton’s The Steakhouse. 

Peter grew up with a twin sister named Pam Morton and a half-brother named Michael Morton. The 75-year-old attended the University of Denver from where he graduated with a BSBA degree. 

The American businessman has been married twice in his life. He first married Paulene Stone and had his son Harry from the relationship. The former pair divorced in 1986.

Peter eventually tied the nuptials with Grace’s mother Tarlton in 1990. The couple was married for seven years before parting ways in 1997.

Morton also has a special connection with the island nation of Jamaica, where his daughter’s wedding was held. According to Vogue UK, Peter lived in Jamaica in the late 1960s.  

Tarlton Pauley Morton

Tarlton was a former art seller and restaurateur. She moved to Los Angeles at the age of 24 and started working out at an art gallery.

The building that she worked at was owned by Peter. Morton noticed her when he was looking out for some ark works to purchase. He immediately approached her for a date and the two soon started dating.

The couple eventually married in 1990 in a lavish wedding and welcomed two children into the family. Their relationship started deteriorating after Grace’s birth in 1994. 

After their divorce in 1997, Tarlton took care of the kids as a single mother. She also struggled with drug addiction during the years following the divorce.

Tarlton passed away at the age of 55 in California on October 26, 2017. Grace dearly missed her mother at her wedding. Meanwhile, her dad and brother Matthew did a great job consoling the bride on her special day.

Morton Married Partner Budman In Jamaica

Grace Morton wedding with Matthew Budman took place in Jamaica in March 2023. 

The couple’s preparation for the wedding started with the hiring of planners Van Wyck and Van Wyck in April 2022. For the wedding, Morton wore a custom dress designed by Chanel featuring two hand-crafted butterflies on her veil. Similarly, Matthew was dressed in a Ralph Lauren Purple Label-designed three-piece tuxedo. 

Grace walked down the aisle with her dad Peter; she dearly missed her mom Tarlton and elder brother Harry on her wedding day. Morton and Budman exchanged their wedding vows under the chuppah prepared for the event.

The Jewish ceremony was attended by several celebrity guests, including Bill Murray, Matthew Vaughn, Nicole Sheridan, Emerson Miller, Ky-Mani Marley, Michael Douglas, Catherine Zeta-Jones, Shay Mitchell, Matte Babel, Fai Khadra, and Joe Keery, among others.

Grace and Matthew first met while working for a production company around 2014. The Bosque Ranch Productions VP was an intern at the company, whereas her partner was a staff member. The duo only started dating after Morton finished her internship.

The couple dated for nearly seven years before getting engaged in September 2021. For the surprise engagement, Matthew took Grace to the Rance located at Rock Creek. He took her to an open field with hundreds of wild horses running around and got down on one knee to propose to her for marriage.

Matthew Budman Is The Producer Of American Hustle

Grace Morton husband Matthew is an American film producer. Budman is best known for working in the 2013 Oscar-nominated film American Hustle.

During his career as a producer extending nearly 15 years, Matthew has worked with famous Hollywood A-listers like Christian Bale, Jeremy Renner, Bradley Cooper, Rami Malek, Margot Robbie, and more. 

Budman commenced his career in the entertainment industry in 2008; he first produced a short film titled The Dreaming. He was also the executive producer of the 2013 movie American Hustle which starred talented actors like Jennifer Lawrence, Amy Adams, Christian Bale, and Michael Pena. 

As of 2023, Matthew has produced 12 projects, according to his IMDb profile. some of his famous movies are Amsterdam, Detroit, Spree, Zero Dark Thirty, Lawless, and Spree.

Moreover, Budman also founded a production company named Forest Hill Entertainment in 2019. Immediately after forming the company, he signed famous musicians Drake and Future The Prince. 

Before founding Forest Hill Entertainment, Matthew worked as a producer at Annapurna Pictures for nearly eight years. 

Regarding his presence on social media, Morton’s husband is not available on platforms like Instagram, Facebook, or Twitter. The producer keeps a low-key profile and stays away from social networking sites.
