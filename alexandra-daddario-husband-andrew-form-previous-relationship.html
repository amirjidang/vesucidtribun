Why Did Andrew Form And Jordana Brewster Get Divorced?

Ever since 2001, there have been a lot of fans who've known Jordana Brewster as one of the Fast and Furious franchise's stars. However, for the film producer Andrew Form, Brewster was his wife from 2007 until 2021 and the mother of his two children.

Related: What Happened To Jordana Brewster? Here’s What She's Been Up To

Throughout Brewster and Form's marriage, they seemed to be very happy. However, after the couple went their separate ways, Glamour published an essay Brewster wrote in which she revealed that she and Form grew apart.

According to Brewster, she developed disordered eating habits which distracted her from what mattered in her life.

"I was so focused on the number on the scale and the number of calories I consumed in a day that I ignored all other problems. I didn’t look closely at my career, my marriage."

Unfortunately for Brewster and Form, one of the truths about her life that she was distracted from was that she and her husband didn't have a true partnership. "But early in our marriage, my husband and I started leading parallel lives."

Once Brewster began to focus on her life instead of her weight, she realized that her marriage was fundamentally flawed and that resulted in her divorce.

"I realized something was missing for both of us. When there wasn’t a project to invest in together, we didn’t have much to say to each other."

"Once I slowed down and got quiet, I could hear myself saying, “We are not on the same page. I can either remain in this comfortable zone and distract myself, or I can face what is not working in my life and fix it.” I chose the latter."

Knowing that Brewster and Form's relationship didn't work because they lacked a connection, that makes you wonder if his new marriage has a similar dynamic.

What Makes Alexandra Daddario And Andrew Form's Marriage So Different?

Thanks to the tabloids, social media, and the fact that stars take part in more interviews than ever before, a lot of fans feel like they understand their favorite stars. In reality, however, there is no way for fans to know what goes on behind closed doors.

Before Daddario and Form walked down the aisle, she spoke to People during their engagement in February of 2022. During that conversation. Daddario expressed that her relationship with Form excites her and puts her at ease at the same time.

"It feels so exciting. But mostly it just feels very peaceful. I feel very at peace with him and very happy, and I just feel very lucky. That's what it just feels. Feels like peace."

On top of what Daddario told People about her relationship with Form, she paid tribute to him on Instagram in 2021. Alongside a photo of herself with Form, Daddario's caption made it pretty clear that, unlike his first wife, she has a tight bond with Form and their love is passionate.
