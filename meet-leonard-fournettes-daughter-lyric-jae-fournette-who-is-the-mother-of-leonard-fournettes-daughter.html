Meet Leonard Fournettes daughter Lyric Jae Fournette; Who Is The Mother Of Leonard Fournettes

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Lyric Jae Fournette, the daughter of Leonard Fournette, is an American young girl who is known to many as the daughter of the American football running back, Leonard Fournette. Lyric Jae Fournette was born in the USA but her exact date of birth has been kept private.

According to reports, Lyric Jae Fournette was born five weeks premature and at the time of birth, she weighed 3 pounds, 2 ounces. Lyric Jae Fournette was born to Leonard Fournette and his long time girlfriend, Jamie Jones.

Leonard Fournette is an American football running back who plies his trade in the National Football League for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers. Leonard Fournette has a brother, Lanard Fournette, who plays college football as a running back for the LSU Tigers of the Louisiana State University.

Leonard Fournette is 27 years of age and he won the 2013 edition of Mr Football Louisiana. Leonard is a one-time Super Bowl champion and the 2013 USA Today′s Offensive Player of the Year.
