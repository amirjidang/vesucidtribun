Actress Ashley Benson is best known for her iconic role as Hanna Marin on the ABC teen drama-thriller series Pretty Little Liars. Her character with the ultra girly, fashion-forward friend in a group of four, who struggle to find out what happened to their friend Alison after she suspiciously disappears. The show, which ran from 2009-2017, was met with much critical acclaim and success. Ashley has been nominated for a variety of awards and nominations for her work on the series; she has won a four Teen Choice Awards, a Young Hollywood Award, a Youth Rock Award, and has been nominated for multiple other Teen Choice Awards, People’s Choice Awards, and MTV Movie Awards.

Since kissing PLL goodbye, Ashley has been featured in a variety of projects that have only worked to increase her star power. She’s made various appearances on well-known TV shows throughout the years, including Punk’d, How I Met Your Mother, the PLL-spinoff Ravenswood, Family Guy, and Barely Famous. In 2010, the celeb starred in the made-for-TV movie Christmas Cupid alongside Christina Milian and Chad Michael Murray. In 2013, she was cast in the highly anticipated flick Spring Breakers, which co-starred James Franco, Vanessa Hudgens, and Selena Gomez. Since then, she has also starred in a handful of other movies, including Ratter (2015), Pixels (2015), Elvis & Nixon (2016), Chronically Metropolitan (2017), and she will star in Her Smell, which is due for release in 2019.

Aside from her work in show business, Ashley has also been adored her for edgy yet fashion-forward style on and off the red carpet. Check out these 25 outfits that will prove that Ashley has amazing style, and will definitely make you miss her face in PLL even more!

blue-eyed and beautiful

Ashley’s character Hanna on Pretty Little Liars was known for her feminine, fashion-forward sense of style. Though Ashley is equally chic in real life, she’s got more of an edgy, rock n’ roll vibe to her style, as evidenced by this all-black outfit. The actress paired a basic leather jacket with a black turtleneck and dark skinny jeans for an A-list event. Her gorgeous baby blues stood out against the dark outfit, while her tousled short ‘do add to the edginess of the look. The silver detailing of her jacket and belt completed the look.

“I had a gothic phase, and now I'm more edgy chic,” the singer has said of her dark sense of style, emphasizing that she’s gone through even edgier phases in her life.

"My all-time fave is Saint Laurent,” she’s also said of her favourite brands and style icons. “I'd wear it 24/7 if I could. Or The Row; I love how Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen mix masculine with feminine." Just like Hanna, Ashley sure has a knack for designer items and putting together stylish outfits. Who knows, maybe she’ll eventually have a career in fashion just like her character did. We know we’d love to shop her own clothing line!
