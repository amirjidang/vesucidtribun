Professional Journey

After completing her studies, Margaret Brennan embarked on a successful career in journalism. She initially worked as a financial journalist for CNBC and Bloomberg Television, where she covered major financial events and provided insightful analysis. Brennan’s expertise and dedication to reporting earned her a reputable position in the industry.

Recognizing Brennan’s remarkable talent, CBS News entrusted her with an even greater responsibility in 2018 when she became the moderator of Face the Nation, the esteemed Sunday morning news and public affairs program. Taking over from John Dickerson, Brennan has continued to deliver thought-provoking interviews and facilitate critical discussions on the nation’s most pressing issues.
