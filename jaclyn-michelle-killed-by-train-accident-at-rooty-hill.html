Jaclyn Michelle Killed By Train Accident At Rooty Hill

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Recently, many accidents have taken place. There are many families that have lost their loved ones in train accidents. 

It is tragic for them, but some of them manage to get back on track to support their family. On the other hand, there are families who lose their father or mother or the bread earner of the family, and they face a lot of challenges ahead. 

A similar case we witnessed when the news broke on social media and local media about the train accident. Jaclyn Michelle, the mother of six children, was walking near Rooty Hill, in Sydney’s west, at about 7.45 pm last Monday.

The incident is unknown, but she accidentally got struck by a train. She was immediately rushed to the hospital and later announced dead. 

The incident was very unfortunate for the children as the family already had the father missing from the family photo. And now, with their mother gone, the six children are suffering.
