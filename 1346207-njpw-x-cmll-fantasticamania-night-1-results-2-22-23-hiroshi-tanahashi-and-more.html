NJPW x CMLL Fantasticamania Night 1 Results (2/22/23): Hiroshi Tanahashi And More

Publish date: 2024-08-31

On February 22, NJPW and CMLL kicked off their Fantasticamania series with the first night of action. The show was held at the Takamatsu City General Gymnasium in Takamatsu, Kagawa, Japan.

The results, courtesy of Fightful, are as follows:

  • Taiji Ishimori & Gedo def. Yuto Nakashima & Capitan Suicida
  • DOUKI & Yoshinobu Kanemaru def. Ryohei Oiwa & Magia Blanca
  • Okumura, Barbaro Cavernario & Hijo de Villano III def. Tiger Mask, YOH & Dulce Gardenia
  • Los Ingobernables de Japon (Hiromu Tanahashi, Tetsuya Naito, BUSHI & Titan) def. Master Wato, Ryusuke Taguchi, Kosei Fujita & Soberano Jr.
  • SHO, Hechicero & Templario def. El Desperado, Volador Jr & Rey Cometa
  • Ultimo Guerrero & Atlantis Jr def. Hiroshi Tanahashi & Mistico
