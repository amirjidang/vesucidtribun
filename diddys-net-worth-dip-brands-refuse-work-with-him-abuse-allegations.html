Diddy Lost His Eyewear Deal

America’s Best Contacts & Eyeglasses, a major eyewear retailer, has decided to stop selling frames from Diddy's Sean John collection. Instructions were reportedly given to stores last week to pull Sean John products from their shelves and replace them with similarly priced alternatives.

The company had already started removing Sean John eyewear from its online store last month, TMZ notes .

This move comes in the wake of public backlash against Diddy after CNN released security camera footage earlier this month showing the rapper assaulting Cassie in a hotel hallway. On May 19, Diddy issued an apology for his actions in a video shared on Instagram, despite denying other claims brought on earlier.

Diddy Remains Under Investigation

In late March, law enforcement authorities conducted raids on his properties, intercepting him while he was on his way to the airport with his daughters for a trip to the Bahamas.
