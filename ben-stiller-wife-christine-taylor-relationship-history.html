Who Did Christine Taylor Date Before Her Relationship With Ben Stiller?

According to E!, Christine Taylor and David Lascher did have a romantic relationship. This revelation came to the limelight during a mini-episode of Hey Dude... The 90's Called! Podcast on December 12, 2022. During the podcast episode, Lascher reminisced about their past, saying, "You were my first real love, and it was full of teen angst and all you can imagine."

He mentioned that people often asked if they dated, and both denied it. However, Taylor confirmed that they did have a relationship, revealing that David Lascher was her "second boyfriend" and that they were "head over heels for each other" off-screen.

Christine Taylor explained that their relationship was characterized by infatuation and strong feelings of love. Their romantic involvement occurred while working together on the set of the '90s sitcom Hey Dude, which aired between 1989 and 1991. The show revolved around teenagers working at the Bar None Dude Ranch in Arizona where Taylor portrayed the bubbly lifeguard Melody Hanson, while Lascher played the charming but trouble-prone Ted McGriff.

Although their young love was intense, Christine Taylor explained that the nature of their work and living situation contributed to the eventual fading of their romance. She mentioned they were constantly together due to work and living in the same hotel, which didn't provide the space necessary for a young relationship to thrive. Despite the challenges, they maintained a strong friendship and shared numerous laughs during their time together.

Actor Matthew Lillard and Christine Taylor also embarked on a short-lived yet noteworthy romantic relationship. The brief union took place in 1995 and left a distinct mark on the personal stories of these two prominent figures.

Lillard etched a notable legacy in film and television and is renowned for his comedic prowess.

When Did Christine Taylor Start Dating Ben Stiller?

According to People, Ben Stiller and Christine Taylor's romantic journey began after their initial meeting while filming a TV pilot named Heat Vision and Jack, which unfortunately never made it to air. In 2016, Ben Stiller revealed that their first meeting during the pilot's audition process quickly transitioned into a first date.

He disclosed, "I was directing a pilot for Fox, starring Jack Black, called Heat Vision and Jack [1999]. My wife came in to audition for the role of the sexy sheriff. She had been in the Brady Bunch movies, and they were pushing her on me. She came in, and she was great … I was single. I'd come out of a long relationship with [actress] Jeanne Tripplehorn. Christine was coming out of a relationship too. We went on a date, and it clicked. We were both on the rebound. And we just jumped."

Their public debut as a couple occurred on June 5, 1999, when Christine Taylor accompanied Ben Stiller to the MTV Movie Awards where he was nominated for four awards for his role in There's Something About Mary. After a six-month engagement, Stiller and Taylor tied the knot on May 13, 2000, in Kauai, Hawaii.

Is Christine Taylor Still Married To Ben Stiller?

Christine Taylor and Ben Stiller are currently still together. After announcing their separation in 2017 after 17 years of marriage, the couple's relationship took a dark turn during the Covid-19 pandemic. During that time, Taylor and Stiller decided to spend time together with their children. This proximity naturally led to a rekindling of their love.

Ben Stiller mentioned in a 2022 interview that their relationship "evolved" over time. He expressed that they were never truly estranged during their separation. The couple attended events together, from the BNP Paribas Open to the US Open and even a Broadway date night.
