Famous for being one of the members of the American R&B group Brownstone, Nicci Gilbert is an American producer, entrepreneur and singer with an estimated net worth of $10 million as of 2012.

The group which earned a great deal of popularity in the 90s is actually composed of three ladies which include Charmayne "Maxee" Maxwell, Nichole "Nicci" Gilbert and Monica "Mimi" Doby. Brownstone is known for their singles titled "Pass the Lovin'", "If You Love Me,"  "Grapevyne" and their beautiful rendition of "I Can't Tell You Why". Their song  "If You Love Me" earned them a Grammy award nomination for the Best R&B Performance category.

As an entrepreneur, Nicci Gilbert is currently running N-Go Entertainment, her own artist development business. She produces TV, film and TV projects. She currently appears on "R&B Divas", a reality TV show airing on TV One.

Nicci Gilbert was born on June 8, 1970 in Detroit Michigan. She is married to Leonard Daniels whom she has a child named Brandy.
