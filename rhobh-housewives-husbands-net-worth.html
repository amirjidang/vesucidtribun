RHOBH: The Housewives' Husbands, Ranked By Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-08-16

The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills has been on air since 2010 and the show is currently in its 10th season. Over the years fans got to witness plenty of different housewives living extremely luxurious and fun lives and while there is no doubt that each of the ladies that has ever been on the show is rich.

Related: Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills: Net Worth Ranking For Every Wife From Season 1 To Now

This list is taking a look at just how rich their husbands are. While fans of the show may be very familiar with Kylie Richards, Lisa Vanderpump, or Dorit Kemsley's husbands — what matters today is just how wealthy each of them is.

10 Vince Van Patten — Net Worth $2 Million

Kicking the list off at spot number 10 is Vince Van Patten, the husband of Eileen Davidson who was one of the main ladies in season five, six, and seven of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Vince Van Patten is an actor and a former professional tennis player who has an estimated net worth of around $2 million. During Eileen's time on the reality television show, fans got to see little of her husband who definitely seemed more private about their life than Eileen!

9 Harry Hamlin — Net Worth $10 Million

Spot number nine on the list goes to Lisa Rinna's husband, Hollywood star Harry Hamlin. As fans of the reality television show already know, Lisa Rina became a main cast member in season five and she is still on the show.

Throughout the past few seasons, fans got to see quite a bit of Harry Hamlin on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills and according to Celebrity Net Worth, his net worth is estimated to be at around $10 million.

8 Donnie Edwards — Net Worth $12 Million

Number eight on this list goes to former professional football player Donnie Edwards who is married to housewife Kathryn Edwards.

While Kathryn was on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills only for season six, her husband has still managed to find his way onto this list as his estimated net worth — according to Celebrity Net Worth — is around $12 million. Just like with Vince Van Patten, fans barely got to see the former athlete on the iconic reality television show.

7 Edwin Arroyave — Net Worth $12 Million

Next on the list is Edwin Arroyave, the husband of Teddi Mellencamp who joined the cast of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills in season eight. According to Celebrity Net Worth, the Colombian-American businessman has a net worth of $12 million which puts him at number seven on this list.

Even though Teddi Mellencamp has been on the show for only three seasons, fans got to see quite a bit of her husband Edwin during that time.

6 Tom Girardi — Net Worth $30 Million

Number six on the list goes to attorney Tom Girardi who is married to housewife Erica Girardi. As fans of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills already know, Erica joined the cast of the popular reality television series in season six and ever since then, fans got to see tiny glimpses of what her life with Tom is really like.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, the 81-year-old attorney has an estimated net worth of around $30 million.

5 Michael Ohoven — Net Worth $50 Million

Opening up the top five richest men of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills is Michael Ohoven. According to Celebrity Net Worth, the German-American film producer has a net worth of around $50 million.

Even though his wife, Joyce Giraud de Ohoven, was only on season four of the popular reality television show, fans still got to see quite a bit of the producer as he would almost always attend all the events and parties with his wife.

4 Paul Kemsley — Net Worth $50 Million

Number four on the list goes to British businessman Paul Kemsley better known as PK who is basically as much on the show as his wife Dorit Kemsley is.

Related: 10 Real Housewives Transformations

According to Celebrity Net Worth, PK is estimated to have a net worth of around $50 million, and considering that Dorit joined the cast of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills in season seven, it’s safe to say that fans got to see quite a bit of PK ever since then.

3 Ken Todd — Net Worth $90 Million

Opening up the top three with an estimated net worth of around $90 million is British businessman and restaurant owner Ken Todd.

Fans of the famous reality television show already know that Lisa Vanderpump’s husband has been a very important cast member of the show from the beginning up until the end of season nine, after which Lisa and Ken decided to not do The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills anymore due to some unforgettable drama that happened in season nine.

2 Mauricio Umansky — Net Worth $100 Million

The runner up on today’s list is Kyle Richards' husband Mauricio Umansky. According to Celebrity Net Worth, the Mexican real estate developer has an estimated net worth of around $100 million and he is definitely the one husband that fans have seen the most of over the years.

Especially considering that Kyle Richards has been a main cast member since the show's premiere in 2010. Throughout the show fans also got to witness plenty of his success in the real estate industry.

1 David Gebbia — Net Worth $150 Million

Wrapping the list up at spot number one is surprisingly yet another husband that fans haven’t seen much of, mainly because his wife hasn’t been on the show for too long.

Yes, Carlton Gebbia was on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills only in season four, however, her husband David — who is a business executive and owner of Stockcross Financial Services — took spot number one on this list because according to Celebrity Net Worth, he is estimated to have a net worth of $150 million.
