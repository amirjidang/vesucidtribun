What Happened To Farruko And Where Is Singer Now Meet His Son Kefren Adiel &amp; Wife Kairy Lopez

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Farruko is a Puerto Rican Christian singer and songwriter who recently spoke profusely about God throughout the concert in Miami.

Carlos Efren Reyes Rosado, whose stage name is Farruko rose to fame after collaborating with heavyweight artists such as Daddy Yankee, J Balvin, Don Omar, and J Alvarez. In 2010, he established himself in the entertainment industry with his debut studio album, El Talento Del Bloque.

His song ‘Pa Romper La Discoteca’ was a big hit, and it was able to increase his fame. He got nominated for the Latin Grammy Award for the Best Urban Music Album in 2012 for his album ‘The Most Powerful Rookie.’

What Happened To Farruko: Where Is Singer Now?

In 2022 the singer Farruko surprised and angered his fans at a concert in Miami, Florida, when he left lyrics about drugs and partying.

He apologized to the audiences for the lyrics and preached about God leaving audiences dumb-fooled. The singer’s act left fans stupefied and questions stirring around about the future of his music career.

The singer said in the concert that he had an encounter with God, the Father, who made him reach deep inside, and this dramatic transformation caught fans off-guard.

The rumors of Farruko’s retirement are also on a buzz. Despite the rumors, his tour is to go on as per schedule, with performances planned for New York, New Jersey, Cleveland, and Rosemont, Illinois.

The singer was born in Bayamon, Puerto Rico, United States, on May 2, 1991, and is currently 30 years old. He belongs to the Puerto Rican ethnicity. He resides in Pureto Ricco and has a brother, Francisco Calderon Rocha.

Farruko launched his debut album in June 2010, which made it into the top 20 lists on the Top Latin Albums competition. The album was a mixture of reggae, R&B, Latin pop, and Latin rock.

The song ‘6 AM’ earned the singer nominations at Premios Juventud for the Best Urban Fusion/Performance and the Best Urban Song at the 15th Annual Latin Grammy Awards.

Farruko has collaborated with many artists, including Bad Bunny, J Balvin, Nicki Minaj, Travis Scott, Ricky Martin, and Wisin and Yandel. He released his solo ‘Pepas’ on June 24, 2021, and has more than 322 million views on YouTube. The song is a combination of Tribal guarachero and reggaeton.

Meet Farruko Children – Son Kefren Adiel & Wife Kairy Lopez

The singer Farruko got married to his longtime girlfriend, Kairy Lopez, and the couple got blessed with three children named Kefren Adiel, Keylon Wayne, and Kaylo Sky.

Kefren Adiel is the second son of Farruko and Kairy Lopez, who was born in 2016.

Kairy Lopez is an entrepreneur who runs the cosmetic line ‘KL THE BRAND,’ her net worth is approximately $5 million, which is just speculation.

Kairy was born in Bayamon, Puerto Rico, on December 30, 1990, but later she migrated to Miami. She grew up with her parents in Bayamon.

She is an Instagram influencer and model. She has a unique appearance and attractive body, which helped her gain various modeling assignments.

The model has over 99.5K followers on Instagram, and she promotes various famous fashion brands on social media and accumulates a decent fortune from them.

The singer Farruko was also in a relationship with Julieselle Machin, and they have two children named Owen and Calier from this affair.
