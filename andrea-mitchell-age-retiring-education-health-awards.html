Andrea Mitchell Age

How old is Andrea Mitchell? Andrea Mitchell was born on October 30, 1946 in New Rochelle, New York, U.S. She is 75 years old.

Andrea Mitchell Education

Andrea Mitchell graduated from New Rochelle High School.[7] She went on to attend the University of Pennsylvania where she received a Bachelor of Arts degree in English literature in 1967

Andrea Mitchell Health

On September 7, 2011, Mitchell disclosed that she had received the news that she had breast cancer a few weeks earlier at a visit to the doctor. Early detection and treatment allowed for the early discovery

Andrea Mitchell Awards

How many awards has Andrea Mitchell won? Mitchell has been nominated for 16 News & Documentary Emmy Awards.

She won the award for Outstanding Coverage of a Breaking News Story in a Regularly Scheduled Newscast for “NBC Nightly News” in 2009 and Outstanding Live Coverage of a Current News Story – Long Form for the NBC News special “Malaysia Flight 17” in 2015.

The News & Documentary Emmys honored Mitchell with Lifetime Achievement Emmy in 2019.
