15 Celebs Who Are Part Of Blake Shelton And Gwen Stefani's Circle (And 5 Who Wish They Were)

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Blake Shelton and Gwen Stefani are two of the biggest names in Hollywood! Not only have the been around for quite some time, but they have both dominated their respective genres unlike any other artist has.

When it comes to country music, Blake Shelton is the biggest name to come to mind, and while Gwen has dabbled in several genres, her days with No Doubt and countless pop hits have made her a notable artist in the music industry.

Considering the two are, without a doubt, a power couple, they surround themselves with the right type of people, many of whom are singers too. Despite their large friend group, the two don't just let anyone into their circle. Here are 15 celebrities that are part of Blake Shelton and Gwen Stefani's circle, and a few that wish they could squeeze in.

39 Trace Adkins

38

Trace Adkins, another country legend, is a very close friend of Blake Shelton's, making him an easy in for him and Gwen's circle. Not only have the two been longtime friends, but they have performed alongside one another on numerous occasions.

37 John Legend

36

John Legend is practically friends with just about everyone, however, he has hit it off quite well with his 'The Voice' co-star, Blake Shelton. Both Blake and Gwen have worked with John Legend, and have become quite close friends since starting the show together.

35 Andy Cohen

34

Andy Cohen is just one of those people who know everyone, including Gwen Stefani! While the two are not the best of friends, they always have a hang out when Stefani visits New York City. Gwen has also starred on Andy's radio show 'Radio Andy' on Sirius XM.

33 Taylor Swift

32

Taylor Swift was once upon a time a country singer prior to turning into a pop sensation, meaning she and Blake Shelton have definitely crossed paths before, however, her friendship with the couple bloomed when she starred on 'The Voice' as the season's mega mentor!

31 Adam Levine

30

Adam Levine is definitely close buds with Blake and Gwen! Not only have they worked together for years on 'The Voice' but they have maintained a close friendship off camera too. They've attended events together, and support each other whenever they can, including at Blake Shelton speaking at Levine's Hollywood Star ceremony.

29 Kelly Clarkson

28

Kelly Clarkson is one person you can't help but be friends with! The first-ever 'American Idol' winner has not only gone on to have a massively successful career in music, but she has also starred alongside Shelton and Stefani on the singing competition. The three got very close on the show, and Clarkson has described them as her "brothers and sisters".

27 Carson Daly

26

Carson Daly has hosted 'The Voice' since day 1 and considering Blake and Gwen have both been coaches for numerous reasons, it is no surprise that Daly has become friends with the couple off-camera too, so much that they are often seen out and about.

25 Katy Perry

24

Katy Perry has definitely made a name for herself in the industry, so much that she and Gwen Stefani have become quite close friends. The two are often spotted attending the same events, parties and celeb gatherings, and look absolutely gorgeous doing so.

23 Keith Urban

22

Keith Urban, another country legend, is also a close friend of the two 'Voice' coaches. Not only have they crossed paths countless times at awards shows and events, but Keith Urban and wife Nicole Kidman have been photographed with Blake and Gwen on several occasions.

21 Pharrell Williams

20

Pharrell Williams is not only a 'Voice' alumn who worked alongside Blake and Gwen on the show, but he is a longtime friend of Gwen's and has worked on a few of her songs. 'Hollaback Girl' is definitely one of Stefani's most popular songs, and it just so happens that she worked very closely with Pharrell on that track.
