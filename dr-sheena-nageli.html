Dr. Sheena Nageli Boerne, TX, Passed Away, What Happened To Her?

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Dr. Sheena Nageli, a cherished member of Boerne Texas and a famous chiropractor, suddenly died. She passed away on Tuesday, 25 April 2023.

The news of her death broke on 26 October on social media, following which a tribute outpouring of condolences from society. Nick Nageli, her husband, shared two days back on Facebook about his feelings on the passing of his wife.

Dr.Sheena Nageli

Dr. Sheena was born in Lubbock, Texas. She spent her early years in Texan town before moving to Boerne and resided there until her death. She was known as the most caring, loving, sincere, strong, empathetic, and respected person, and she will always be missed by those who shared their moments with her.

Dr Sheena Nageli’s education

Dr. Sheena received her doctorate in exercise physiology from the University of Oklahoma. She earned a bachelor’s degree in Human Anatomy and a bachelor’s degree in Health and wellness from Parker College of Chiropractic in Dallas, Texas.

Parker College of Chiropractic awarded her a doctorate as a board-certified chiropractor in 2009. She took a few months off after graduation to spend time with her sister, who was expecting her second child.

Dr. Sheena was tremendously impacted by the experience of aiding with the birth of her new nephew.  Dr. Sheena shifted her clinical focus to pregnant and pediatric chiropractic therapy as a result of this experience.

Dr.Sheena Career

Dr. Sheena’s interest in chiropractic developed when she underwent chiropractic treatment at age 17, and the positive consequences of the treatment made her pursue her career as a chiropractor.

After completing her education, she started her training in pediatrics and prenatal care at the organizations that provide chiropractor care. The three organizations are the International Chiropractic Pediatric Association (ICPA), EPIC Pediatrics, and the Pediatric Experience.

She recently studied to become an IBCLC to provide care and support for postpartum patients. She was also a certified birth doula, and after completing her diploma, she was involved in the birth of her nephew; her successful experience made her concentrate her chiropractic practice on pregnancy and Pediatric patient care to provide peaceful delivery and postpartum lives.

Gofundme

A GoFundMe was organized on 27 April Thursday to help Dr. Sheena’s family with the arrangements for the funeral.
