Caring's Caprice Holdings, which runs London restaurants including The Ivy, J Sheekey, and Le Caprice, made a £8.4m profit on sales of £39.4m in 2008-09.

This individual also owns International Clothing Designs (Holdings), a London-based supplier of fashion garments. He made his initial fortune in the 1980s and 1990s through property deals in Hong Kong.
