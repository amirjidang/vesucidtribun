Ireland Baldwin Split From Angel Haze Before Checking Into Rehab

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Ireland Baldwin and Angel Haze are no longer an item. The model, 19, and the rapper, 23, quietly split shortly before Baldwin checked into rehab for "emotional trauma," a source confirms to Us Weekly. However, one isn't necessarily the cause of the other.

"This was not the reason Ireland entered a multi-treatment facility," the source tells Us of the breakup. "She entered due to a variety of pressures in her life. Ireland is being pro-active, doing really well, and being positive about life."

Baldwin — the daughter of actors Alec Baldwin and ex-wife Kim Basinger — was first linked with Haze back in May 2014, when she shared a photo of them kissing on Cinco de Mayo. Their relationship was later confirmed even more explicitly in The Independent's interview with the rapper, who has identified as agender on Twitter and prefers the gender-neutral pronoun "they."

"An interracial gay couple, I mean, that's just weird for America right now," Haze said at the time. "We f–k and friends don't f–k. I have never f–ked one of my friends." 

The two made their red carpet debut as a couple just a few months later in August, at the 2014 MTV Video Music Awards. More recently, though, Haze has been diving into work while hinting at personal troubles on Twitter.
