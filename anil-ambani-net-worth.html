Anil Ambani is an Indian business tycoon who has an estimated net worth of $3.1 billion as of April 2016, according to Forbes. He currently serves as the Chairman of one of the largest privately held conglomerates in India, the Anil Dhirubhai Ambani Group. He belongs to the wealthiest family in India which is also considered as one of the richest in the world. The family's current wealth is primarily inherited from the founder of the largest conglomerate the Reliance Group. He first joined the Reliance Industries in 1983 as the Co-Chief Executive Officer. Today, Reliance Group is India's leading petroleum, textile, power, telecom and petrochemicals company. He is said to top the list of "world's biggest loser" by Business Sheets after $32 billion when the recession occurred  in 2008.
