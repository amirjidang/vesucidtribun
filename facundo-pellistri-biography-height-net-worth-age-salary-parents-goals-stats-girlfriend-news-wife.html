Biography

Facundo Pellistri Rebollo (born December 20, 2001), hailing from Uruguay, is a versatile professional footballer. 

Known for his position as a right winger, he exhibits his talents on the field for Premier League club Manchester United and when representing his country in the Uruguay national team. Pellistri possesses impressive attributes as a winger, including speed, proficient dribbling abilities, and a keen sense for scoring opportunities. 

Although he is still in the early stages of his career and undergoing development, Facundo Pellistri has great potential to emerge as a standout player for Manchester United and the Uruguayan national team.

Career

Facundo Pellistri embarked on his professional football journey in 2018 with Peñarol. In August 2019, he made his inaugural appearance in professional football, quickly establishing himself as a crucial team member with exceptional skills. Pellistri‘s remarkable contributions were duly recognized when he received the prestigious South American Young Player of the Year award in 2020, solidifying his position as an emerging star.

In a significant move, Pellistri transferred to Manchester United for £10 million in September 2020. Despite the anticipation, his debut for the club came in January 2023. During his debut season, he made 10 appearances for the team. However, a few months later, Pellistri experienced a loan spell at the Spanish club Deportivo Alavés in the same year.

His time on loan proved fruitful, showcasing his talent with an impressive 33 appearances for Alavés in the 2022–23 season, accompanied by a goal.

Upon returning to Manchester United in July 2022, Pellistri actively contributed to the club in the 2022–23 season, participating in eight matches and scoring one goal.

Facundo Pellistri‘s prowess extends to the international stage, representing Uruguay. Making his senior debut in January 2022, he was selected for the Uruguay team in the FIFA World Cup in 2022. Notably, he featured in Uruguay’s inaugural tournament match against South Korea.
