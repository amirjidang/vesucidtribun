Johnny Gargano And Candice LeRae Welcome Their Baby Into The World In Marvel-ous Fashion

Publish date: 2024-08-14

The wait for and Candice LeRae‘s newborn has finally come to an end as Baby Wrestling has arrived.

As Johnny Gargano revealed this morning on social media, Candice gave birth to their son Quill Gargano on February 17.

If the name sounds familiar to you, there’s a reason for that…

Quill is the last name of Marvel superhero Star-Lord, whose real name is Peter Quill. The leader of The Guardians of the Galaxy.

The Garganos have wasted no time introducing their son to the world of Marvel as the photo Johnny released on Twitter this morning showcases Quill in Nova Corps clothing with his name on it. Nearby he has a plush Rocket Racoon, a Guardians of the Galaxy comic book, and a cassette tape with his name on it, similar to Peter Quill’s “Awesome Mix” of music that can be seen in the Marvel Studios Guardians of the Galaxy franchise.  You can check out the image of Quill in the embedded tweet below.
