Tyler Hoechlin Brother Tanner Hoechlin Is A Family Man

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Tyler Hoechlin brother Tanner Hoechlin is an American actor. Tyler and Tanner appeared together in the movie 7th Heaven.

The actor has a lovely family and a close connection with his siblings and nieces. Unlike him and Tanner, his other family members have different career choices.

However, they all share an interest in sports and support the University of Southern California team Trojans.

Tyler Hoechlin Brother Tanner Keeps A Low Profile

Tyler Hoechlin brother Tanner Hoechlin is a former baseball player and actor. Tanner is the younger sibling and is 33 years old.

Tanner was born in Corona, California, United States, on May 27, 1989. He is two years younger than the Teen Wolf star.

The sibling made an acting debut in a 1998 video called Disney Sing Along Songs: Happy Haunting Party at Disneyland.

In the short comedy family video, he played the role of Halloween Partygoer, whereas his sibling played Zach.

He worked alongside Isaiah Griffin, Rachel Talbott, Murry Phillips, Troy Magino, Yvette Mayes, Stella Choe, Leilani Copp, Peter Duda, Wayne Allwine, Russi Taylor, and more in Gred Gold-directed movies.

Besides, he got called to play the role of Michael Sullivan Jr. However, it is portrayed by Tyler in Road to Perdition, as the show caster decided to use him instead. Through the show, he has earned recognization.

Afterward, he played with Tyler in the 7th Heaven in one episode of Major League as a Guy 1 in 2004.

Besides acting, he has a keen interest in sports, baseball, and golf. He studied at Santiago High School and completed his graduation in 2007.

He was a member of the Santiago High School baseball team Santiago Sharks, in Corona, CA.

He has played on two baseball teams covered by MaxPreps. He played 24 overall games and made a batting average of 351 with 26 hits.

The former baseball player used to wear 15 number jersey and played in the INF position as a senior player. He used to do batting, Baserunning, and Fielding.

In the match between St. Louis Cardinals and Cleveland Indians, he supported his buddy Joe kelly for his MLB debut on June 10, 2012.

Even he went to the stadium to watch the baseball pitcher game in September 2012. He went to watch the game in San Diego with his siblings.

They watched the Petco Park pirates, Halos post-season, Dodger pre-game, and Jacky Line game.

The actor has two other siblings, Travis and Carrie. Unlike Tyler, his other brother is an entrepreneur.

He engaged in the strategies of improving the quality of cases and generating revenue and higher retainers.

Besides business, he was also involved in sports like baseball and golfing. He even had a great time Golfing with his friends in Myrtle Beach in Myrtle Beach, SC.

The Teen Wolf Star Was Raised in California

Tyler Hoechlin was born to parents Don and Lori Hoechlin in Corona, California. According to the actor, his family is Native American, German, Irish, and others.

His parents took their grandkids zip-lining for the first time at Provo Canyon Adventures in August 2022.

Father Donald R. Hoechlin, MD

The actor’s father, Don, is an emergency medicine physician who served at Los Angles Community Hospital in Norwalk, CA.

He has more than 21 years of experience in the field. He graduated from the University of California, Irvine, School of Medicine, with a medical degree in 1977. Later, he joined Yale-New Haven Medical Center for an internship.

He specializes in emergency medicine diagnosing and treating patients who suffered from a heart attack, shock, drug overdose, and massive bleeding.

Mother Lori Partridge Hoechlin

The actor’s mother, Lori, is a racer. She raced in the Women Senior Open Cat 5 category. She appeared at SLR, Road Race, for Sisterhood of Cycling, and the 2018 Subaru Sea Otter Classic-Road Race for Rancho Mission Viejo, CA.

She raced at the San Dimas Stage Race, SLR Road Race, Tour of the Gila, 2019 Sea Otter Classic- Road, and Santa Barbara Country Road Race. Besides racing, golfing is her other passion.
