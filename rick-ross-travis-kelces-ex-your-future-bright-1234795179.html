Rick Ross Tells Travis Kelce's Ex, &quot;Your Future Bright&quot;

Publish date: 2024-08-09

Rick Ross has no shame when it comes to telling a beautiful woman that he admires her. Kayla Nicole, ex-girlfriend to Travis Kelce of the Kansas City Chiefs, is no exception.

The Biggest Boss decided to slide under a two-month old photo of Kayla in a tiny black bikini, with the comment, “Your future bright;” a phrase that he’s said time and time before.

Immediately, fans began claiming that Rozay was shooting his shot at the model and sports correspondent. “You shooting your shot?” one person wrote under his comment as another said, “Take her to the promise land boss.”

Nicole has been in headlines lately, as social media has been comparing her to the tight end’s new rumored partner, Taylor Swift. Nicole dated the Super Bowl champion inconsistently for five years before calling it it quits in May 2022.

Back in 2017, the 33-year-old spoke with E. News! on how he pulled the 31-year-old. “I didn’t just go through every single pic and just start liking it immediately. It was over the course of a month’s span, just following her and liking the pictures that she was posting and watching all of her stories when stories had first started coming out.”

Admitting to “stalking” her, he added, “I was just stalking her and then finally on New Year’s, she gave in. And, you know, New Year new me. She just shot her shot, jumped in my DMs and the rest is history.”

Ross, on the other hand, hasn’t been too specific about with who he’s seriously involved with. However, he and comedian Pretty Vee were reportedly dating last December.

“I think it’s the boss in him,” Vee told REVOLT’s Black Girl Stuff talk show about what she likes about the Miami rapper. “I just think he’s like, you know, I like guys who lead. So when you’re a leader, you can step to me, you know?”

“But again, when it comes to Ross, Ross is the type of person who you look at and you just admire, you’re inspired by. Like, I wanna do what you’re doing, so teach me.”
