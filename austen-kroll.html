Austen Kroll News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Name:Austen Kroll

Relationship:Single

Birthday:June 16, 1987

About

Austen Kroll joined Bravo’s Southern Charm cast in 2017 during season 4. He appeared in the Southern Charm and Summer House crossover, Winter House, in 2021.

The Kings Calling Brewing Company founder launched his own craft beer called Trop Hop in 2019. The University of Alabama graduate went on to host the “Pillows and Beers” podcast with costar Craig Conover.

Kroll briefly dated Southern Charm alum Chelsea Meissner during season 4 of the reality series in 2017 before taking up with the stylist’s friend Victoria Bolyard in season 5. His on-again, off-again romance with Southern Charm‘s Madison LeCroy began during the filming of season 6, but they officially called it quits in December 2020 after two years of dating.
