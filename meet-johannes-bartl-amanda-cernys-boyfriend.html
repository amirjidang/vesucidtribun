Meet Johannes Bartl, Amanda Cernys Boyfriend

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Johannes Bartl is a certified fitness model and athlete who is also well known as a social media star, YouTuber, and actor.

Johannes Bartl was Born in Vienna, Austria on 29th October 1987. Bartl was single mothered by his mother Gabi Bartl.

He has more than 1.9 million followers on Instagram and around 430k subscribers across his YouTube channel.

Bartl’s website, Body By Bartl, mentions that he began his journey in fitness at the tender age of 12 years when he started doing push-ups, sit-ups, and pull-ups as a part of the soccer, swim, and volleyball team.

Johannes Bartl stands at a height of 5 ft 10 inches. As of 2020, he weighs 82 kg.

Johannes Bartl Girlfriend

Bartl is in a relationship with model and social media personality Amanda Cerny. Cerny is an American star who rose to fame on the Vine social media platform and later established herself as an Instagram model.

Bartl and Cerny started dating in 2017, however, they kept their romance out of media sight for months.
