Jake Paul And Alissa Violet's Accusations Of Infidelity In Their Controversial Relationship

Jake Paul's relationships have often been marred by controversy, but perhaps none more so than his tumultuous romance with fellow YouTuber Alissa Violet. Their year-long relationship while they were both members of Team 10 was fraught with accusations of infidelity and public drama.

In 2017, the drama reached a boiling point when Jake accused Alissa of cheating on him with his older brother and fellow boxer, Logan Paul. Jake's tweets sparked a media frenzy with fans taking sides, and he later released a diss track "It's Everyday Bro," which fans interpreted as a jab at Alissa.
