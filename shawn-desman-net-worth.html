Shawn Desman is the stage name of Canadian pop/R&B singer of Portuguese descent who has an estimated net worth of $6 million. He was born Shawn Bosco Fernandes on January 12, 1982 in Vaughan, Ontario, Canada. He started singing in his Portuguese/Italian communities at the young age of 9 years old. At the age of 12, he started taking dance classes. Shortly after, because of his immense talent, he taught his own dance classes.

When Shawn was 16, he decided to take his singing career to a whole new level. He then recorded demo-tapes and sent them to various major record labels. After a while, he got discovered by his manager Camara Alford for BMG. After his discovery, he opened up a subdivision of BMG called UOMO, which houses many Canadian artists, like rising star Keshia Chante and In Essence. He got to work with Rodney Jerkins while recording his single Sexy for the movie Honey where he also had an appearance.

Shawn Desman is not only known in the music business but also in the movie business. He appeared in the movie Get Over It which features Kirsten Dunst, Sisqó and more. He is not only trying to get his music to the ears of the whole world but is also presently writing/producing for Canadian artist Keshia Chante.
