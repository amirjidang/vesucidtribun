American pop singer, songwriter, actress, model, and television personality, Katharine McPhee, has an estimated net worth of $6 million. Katharine McPhee's classic good looks and voice and affinity for traditional pop made her a strong contender throughout the fifth season of the Fox reality show American Idol in 2006. After the American Idol season ended, McPhee was signed by show creator Simon Fuller's 19 Recordings Limited label and released her first single, Somewhere Over the Rainbow/My Destiny; it became the second best-selling single of 2006.

In addition to releasing three albums, McPhee has also established an acting career, the Season Five runner-up has co-starred in series "The House Bunny" and is set to co-star in NBC’s "Smash". Born Katharine Hope McPhee on March 25, 1984 in Los Angeles, California. In 2006, McPhee founded a charity called McPhee Outreach, the purpose of the charity is to team up with other foundations or organizations and help in any way possible.
