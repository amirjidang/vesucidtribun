Glaser attended the prestigious Buckingham Browne & Nichols School and later enrolled at the Cambridge School of Weston, where his passion for English and theater began to take shape. Eager to further explore his interests, he pursued a degree in English and theater at Tulane University. This educational experience provided Glaser with a solid academic background and a deeper understanding of storytelling and performance.

Continuing his pursuit of excellence, Glaser went on to earn a master’s degree in acting and directing from Boston University.
