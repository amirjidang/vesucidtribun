The 10 Highest Grossing Amber Heard Films, Ranked

American film and television actress Amber Heard rose to fame with her roles in the films Pineapple Express and Never Back Down. She is currently all over the news because of the legal battle with her ex-husband Johnny Depp. Before she was even put in the hot seat with numerous negative claims towards her by her former husband, she starred in numerous successful films. She first started in the movie industry in 2006 starring on the film titled All the Boys Love Mandy Lane. Among her highest grossing films are listed below.

The Stepfather

The Stepfather is a film released on October 16, 2009 and was distributed by Screen Gems and Sony Pictures Releasing. The film had about $29,062,561 domestic box office results and $29,227,561 worldwide box office results. The movie directed by Nelson McCormick is about Michael Harding played by Penn Badgley who returned home from the military school and discovered that his mother played by Sela Ward has a new boyfriend named David played by Dylan Walsh. Michael has seen his mother so much happier with David around but he cannot shake off the unpleasant he feels towards David.

3 Days to Kill

3 Days to Kill is a film released on February 21, 2014 and was distributed by EuropaCorp and Relativity Media. The film had about $30,697,999 domestic box office results and $38,959,900 worldwide box office results. The movie directed by McG is about a spy named Ethan Renner played by Kevin Costner who is facing a terminal illness and decided to just give up his dangerous life to make amends and repair his relationship with his estranged wife and daughter. However in order to be able to do that, he needs to apprehend one of the world's most ruthless terrorists.

Never Back Down

Never Back Down is a film released on March 14, 2008 and was distributed by Summit Entertainment and FilmFlex. The film had about $24,850,922 domestic box office results and $39,319,801 worldwide box office results. The movie directed by Jeff Wadlow is about a man named Jake played by Sean Faris who moved to Orlando, Florida with his mother played by Leslie Hope and brother played by Wyatt Smith. Upon moving, he feels as if he was an outsider in his new school and just lets his temper get in the way. With his determination to fit in, he decided to a party with his classmate Baja played by Amber Heard. He got into a fight and was humiliated in front of his classmates; this has pushed him to learn martial arts.

Drive Angry

Drive Angry is a film released on February 25, 2011 and was distributed Warner Bros. Pictures, Lionsgate and Summit Entertainment. The film had about $10,721,033 domestic box office results and $41,042,583 worldwide box office results. The movie directed by Patrick Lussier is about a man who escaped from hell in order to find the killers of his daughter and rescue his granddaughter at the same time.

The Danish Girl

The Danish Girl is a film released on November 27, 2015 and was distributed by Lionsgate and Universal Pictures. The film had about $11,114,018 domestic box office results and $66,542,904 worldwide box office results. The movie directed by Tom Hooper is about an artist named Einar Wegener played by Eddie Redmayne who has the support of his loving wife Gerda played by Alicia Vikander to undergo one of the first ever sex-change operations.

Zombieland

Zombieland is a film released on October 2, 2009 and was distributed by Columbia Pictures and Sony Pictures Releasing. The film had about $75,590,286 domestic box office results and $102,236,596 worldwide box office results. The movie directed by Ruben Fleischer is about a virus which can turn people into zombies. The humans had to survive the ongoing battle against zombies. There are only four survivors left, Tallahassee played by Woody Harrelson, Columbus played by Jesse Eisenberg, Wichita played by Emma Stone and Little Rock played by Abigail Breslin.

Pineapple Express

Pineapple Express is a film released on August 6, 2008 and was distributed by Columbia Pictures and Sony Pictures Releasing. The film had about $87,341,380 domestic box office and $102,404,019 worldwide box office results. The movie directed by David Gordon is about a stoner Dale Denton played by Seth Rogen who loves to use a rare strain of marijuana which has put him in a dangerous position when he witnessed a crime scene. When he realized that the weed can be traced back to him, Dale together with his dealer played by James Franco went on a lam with a dangerous drug lord played by Gary Cole.

Magic Mike XXL

Magic Mike XXL is a film released on July 1, 2015 and was distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures. The film had about $66,013,057 domestic box office results and $123,580,421 worldwide box office results. The movie directed by Gregory Jacobs is about the life of Mike Lane played by Channing Tatum three years after he retired from stripping. He does miss dancing and the excitement of performing on stage. He also misses his old crew called the Kings of Tampa. When an opportunity came knocking to him to go back to stripping once again, he just could not resist the temptation to join.

Justice League

Justice League is a film released on November 17, 2017 and was distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures. The film had about $229,024,295 domestic box office results and $229,024,295 worldwide box office results. The movie directed by Zack Snyder is about Bruce Wayne facing an even greater threat after he formed an alliance with Diana Prince. Inspired and fueled by the selfless act by Superman, his faith in humanity was restored. He decided to form and recruit league of heroes in order to defeat the enemies.

Aquaman

Aquaman is a film released on December 21, 2018 and was distributed by Warner Bros. Pictures. The film had about $335,061,807 domestic box office results and $1,143,758,700 worldwide box office results. The movie directed by James Wan is about the power-hungry King Orm who wants to conquer the world with the remaining oceanic people. He wants to conquer the city of Atlantis which is the most advanced civilization on Earth.
