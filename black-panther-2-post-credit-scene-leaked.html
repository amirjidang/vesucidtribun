Black Panther 2 Post Credit Scene Leaked Here Is What We Know

Publish date: 2024-08-11

The mysterious second Black Panther 2 credits scene leaked has buzzed the internet. 

An alleged Wakanda Forever storyline leak has caused a flurry of discussion regarding Black Panther 2 spoilers.

Given the year that Marvel films and TV series have had, Ryan Coogler’s Black Panther 2: Wakanda Forever, the final movie that Marvel will give us in 2022, is possibly the best we could’ve hoped for.

The fact that Black Panther: Wakanda Forever will only have a mid-credits scene suggests that the highly anticipated superhero movie would end with a homage to the late star Chadwick Boseman.

Big Screen Leaks claims that Marvel Studios intentionally chose to include a mid-credits sequence in Black Panther: Wakanda Forever.

Nothing about the mid-credits sequence has been confirmed. However, it’s assumed that Marvel took this action in an effort to honor Boseman, who played Black Panther in the first 2018 movie. 

A similar part would replace the post-credits scene customary for Marvel movies.

According to reports, Black Panther 2 would include two credit scenes, and Marvel might want to keep the second one’s reveal a mystery.

The alternative theory is that Marvel chose to skip or postpone the unveiling in the other scene for a later adventure. 

After explaining what occurs in the first Wakanda Forever credits scene, we’ll talk about the eagerly awaited second scene.

There have been numerous rumors recently asserting that Doctor Doom is not in Wakanda Forever. And that meant the highly anticipated villain wouldn’t appear in the credits scenes.

The second, the more thrilling idea is that Marvel will include a second credits scene in the movie as a great surprise. But you shouldn’t have high expectations. 

Doom in Wakanda Forever is optional for the sequel to be a terrific film.

According to earlier Wakanda Forever leaks, Doctor Doom will play the role of the movie’s true antagonist, the mastermind behind the war between Wakanda and Namor, Tenoch Huerta. 

The rumors also claimed that the film’s closing credits scenes would introduce us to the eagerly anticipated lousy guy.

Doctor Doom will show up in the first credits scene, but Nakia’s pregnancy will be highlighted in the second, according to the comprehensive plot leak from July.

About Black Panther 2

Black Panther 2 will hit theatres later this year, and we’ve seen a couple of rounds of footage, enough to ferret out some information about the hotly anticipated sequel.

Following the terrible passing of Chadwick Boseman in August 2020, the first trailer, which was released on July 23 at Comic-Con during Marvel’s epic presentation, announced that T’Challa’s death would be a part of the plot.

Since then, another teaser has been released, which means there are more to look at for hints as to what the sequel will be about and who will be playing the eponymous superhero. 

Black Panther: Wakanda Forever will now be released in theatres on November 11, 2022, following the delay in October 2021.

The sequel now airs in July 2023, replacing The Marvels, which was initially scheduled for July 2022.

This is a four-month delay. We hope this will be the final shift for the film, but something is still being determined at this point.
