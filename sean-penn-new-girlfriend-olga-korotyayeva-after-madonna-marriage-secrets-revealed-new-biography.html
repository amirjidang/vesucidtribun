Sean Penn Steps Out With Much-Younger Girlfriend After Shocking Secrets From Madonna Marriage Reveal

Highlights

Sean Penn recently stepped out with his new girlfriend Olga Korotyayeva amid new revelations about his decade-past marriage to Madonna, who recently embarked on her Celebration would tour this month.

Sean, 63, and Olga, 43, were first linked in May 2023 when they were seen going for dinner in Malibu. Since then, they’ve been traveling everywhere from Ukraine to France, with the pair most recently photographed at the Nice airport having a touching moment.

Sean’s Relationship With Olga Comes After His Marriage To Leila George Fell Apart

On October 16, photographers caught Sean waiting at the airport to pick Olga up after she touched down. The couple shared a sweet kiss before going on their way, photos published by Daily Mail show. Sean has clearly moved on from his brief marriage to Animal Kingdom star Leila George. The pair married in 2020, only for Leila to file for divorce the following year, citing irreconcilable differences.

Speaking to Hollywood Authentic, Sean later admitted that he’d been “neglectful” to his wife during their first year of marriage and takes credit for their break-up.

“There's a woman who I'm so in love with, Leila George, who I only see on a day-to-day basis now, because I f***ed up the marriage,' the actor said. “We were married technically for one year, but for five years, I was a very neglectful guy.”

Ultimately, however, the pair failed to reconcile and their divorce was granted in 2022. Olga is the first person Sean has been romantically linked to since the split, though his other ex-wife, House of Cards star Robin Wright, denied rumors she was reconciling with Sean in January after they were seen getting close at LAX.

Sean and Olga remain private about their relationship. She’s also an actress, with roles in films like Banking District, and in La vérité si je mens! 3. Her most recent credit is a film from 2021 called Blast.

Sean And Madonna Haven’t Responded To Startling Accusations About Their Marriage

Sean has been married three times in the past. In addition to his unions to Leila and Robin, his first marriage was to singer Madonna. The pair were wed for several years between 1985 to 1989, and the relationship was allegedly tumultuous with accusations of abuse.

A new biography recently made a variety of claims about Sean and Madonna’s marriage. In an excerpt from the book written by Mary Gabriel, The Times reveals that Sean struggled with Madonna’s fame, as their marriage came right when her music career was taking off.

"Their expectations for their relationship had also diverged. She wanted a marriage of equals. He had a strong streak of traditionalism that required him to be the 'man,’” Gabriel writes.

The author goes on to claim the couple’s arguments grew more intense with time, especially as Sean reportedly grappled with alcohol.

Despite the seriousness of the claims, neither Sean nor Madonna has addressed Gabriel’s new biography.
