Pre-Order Rihannas Unapologetic Deluxe Album For $250

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Rihanna’s seventh studio album Unapologetic is set to drop on November 16, but loyal members of the Rihanna Navy can pre-order a special deluxe package of the album for a pretty penny: $250.

That may seem like a lot to shell out for a pre-order, but it seems like you’ll get a lot for your dollar.

Aside from the actual album, the Diamonds Executive Platinum Box includes a handwritten personal note from Rih Rih, a custom-made white-and-silver View-Master with a “vintage reel of never-before-released 3-D images” of her style evolution, a t-shirt, a two-gigabyte flash drive with her photo on it, seven art-print lithographs, seven laptop stickers, a 40-page book of notes and lyrics, a poster, a 20-minute DVD, and a special-edition vinyl record featuring remixes of the hit “Diamonds.”

If you do make the investment, the hefty package will ship on Dec. 11. The pre-order is available on Rihanna’s official site here.
